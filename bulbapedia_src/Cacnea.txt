'''Cacnea''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''サボネア''' ''Sabonea'') is a {{type|Grass}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s into {{p|Cacturne}} starting at [[level]] 32.

==Biology==
Cacnea is a green, bipedal {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a round body and cactus-like striations. It has a dark green rhombus shape above its face, and six dark green rhombus shapes that ring around its waist. It has dark circles around its eyes, and its mouth composed of several similar circles. It has two dark green spikes on each side of its head, along with a yellow flower bud on top of its head. It has two club-like arms that are ringed with dark green spikes, and its two stubby legs are dark green and conical.

Cacnea uses its thorny arms like hammers to attack its opponents. Prior to [[Generation V]], {{m|Needle Arm}} was its [[signature move]]. It is able to live in {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Rough-terrain Pokémon|deserts}} for 30 days without any water, due to its body being able to store moisture. The beauty and fragrance of its yellow flower depend on the harshness of its environment. This flower releases a strong aroma to attract prey. When prey approaches, Cacnea shoots sharp thorns to bring it down.

===Evolution===
Cacnea evolves into {{p|Cacturne}}.

===Major appearances===
====[[James's Cacnea]]====
Cacnea debuted in ''[[AG006|A Poached Ego!]]''. [[James]] caught it after he had to release his {{TP|James|Weezing}}, alongside Jessie's {{TP|Jessie|Arbok}}, to protect a group of {{p|Ekans}} and {{p|Koffing}} that were freed from [[Rico|a Pokémon Poacher]]. In ''[[DP054|Once There Were Greenfields]]'', James left Cacnea in the care of [[Gardenia]], who agreed to train it to master {{m|Drain Punch}}.

===Minor appearances===
A Trainer's Cacnea appeared in ''[[JN141|A Squad's Worth of Passion!]]''.

A Cacnea appeared in ''[[HZ011|Arboliva's Forest]]''.

A Cacnea appeared in [[HZ090]].

==Trivia==
* No other Pokémon has the same [[Egg Group]] combination as Cacnea and its Evolution.
** Cacnea and its Evolution are also the only {{type|Grass}} Pokémon in the {{egg3|Human-Like}}.
* Cacnea shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Maractus}}. They are both known as the Cactus Pokémon.
* Cacnea has no [[Egg Moves]] in {{g|Scarlet and Violet}} prior to Version 2.0.1. This was presumably unintentional, as it regained them in said version. The same situation occurred with {{p|Luvdisc}} and [[Alolan form|Alolan]] {{p|Grimer}}.

===Origin===
Cacnea may be based on a {{wp|barrel cactus}} and possibly a {{wp|jack-o'-lantern}}. The flower on top of its head resembles a {{wp|crown}}.

====Name origin====
Cacnea may be a combination of ''cactus'' and its Japanese name.

Sabonea may be a combination of 仙人掌 ''saboten'' (cactus) and ''debonair''.
