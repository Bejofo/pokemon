'''Camerupt''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''バクーダ''' ''Bakuuda'') is a dual-type {{2t|Fire|Ground}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s from {{p|Numel}} starting at [[level]] 33.

Camerupt can [[Mega Evolution|Mega Evolve]] into '''Mega Camerupt''' using the [[Cameruptite]].

==Biology==
Camerupt is a quadrupedal {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} that resembles a {{wp|Bactrian camel}}. It has two gray volcanoes on its back, which are bigger on a female's back than those on a male. It also has a stout body with red shaggy fur and three blue ring-shaped markings on both sides. It has bangs on top of its head, droopy ears, and a tan fur-less muzzle.

Its body contains amounts of molten lava of 18,000&nbsp;°F (10,000&nbsp;°C), which Camerupt voluntarily erupts out of its humps if it is angered.<ref>Stated in Camerupt's [[Pokédex entry|Pokédex entries]] for {{game|Emerald}} and {{game|HeartGold and SoulSilver|s}}.</ref> Otherwise, its humps erupt automatically every ten years.<ref>Stated in Camerupt's [[Pokédex entry|Pokédex entries]] for {{game|Ruby and Sapphire|s|Pokémon Sapphire}}, {{game|FireRed and LeafGreen|s}}, and {{g|Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire}}, as well as in dialogue in the {{g|Mystery Dungeon series}}, and shown in the {{pkmn|animated series}}.</ref> As shown in the {{aniseries|AG}} episode ''[[AG046|Candid Camerupt!]]'', it can become uncontrollably angry when it is struck in the face. It lives in the {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Mountain Pokémon|craters of volcanoes}}.

===Forms===
Camerupt can [[Mega Evolution|Mega Evolve]] into Mega Camerupt.

As Mega Camerupt, the humps on its back have transformed into one large and very active volcano. Its fur expands and grows to a point where only its hooves can be seen, and its ears and the tufts on its head grow thicker and longer, standing on end. It gains a gray M on the front of its face and loses the three blue rings on its body. 

Magma is constantly bubbling up from within its body. It has a deep hatred of water or any moisture, with an explosive personality, and a short temper. The volcano on its back erupts constantly in an effort to intimidate its foes.<ref name= "Mega">http://www.pokemonrubysapphire.com/en-us/pokemon/new-mega-evolutions/mega-camerupt</ref>

====Major appearances====
Camerupt made its animated series debut in ''[[AG046|Candid Camerupt!]]'', under the ownership of [[Winstrate family|Vicky Winstrate]]. Vicky used the Eruption Pokémon to {{pkmn|battle}} [[Ash's Pikachu]]. Once Pikachu struck Camerupt on the nose with {{m|Iron Tail}}, it went on an {{m|Eruption}} rampage. Vito's {{p|Alakazam}} used {{m|Rain Dance}} in order to stop Camerupt before it could reach the Winstrate's vegetable patch.

In ''[[AG086|Lights, Camerupt, Action!]]'', [[Elijah]] used a Camerupt to help carry his filmmaking equipment during his trips to small towns all over [[Hoenn]].

In ''[[DP140|Historical Mystery Tour!]]'', a Camerupt attacked {{Ash}}, {{an|Dawn}}, and {{an|Barry}}. However, the crisis was resolved when the trio decided to attack the rings on its side.

In ''[[M19|Volcanion and the Mechanical Marvel]]'', a Camerupt that can [[Mega Evolution|Mega Evolve]] was under the control of [[Levi]] and [[Cherie]]'s Mega Wave. Camerupt was used to battled [[Ash's Noivern]] and {{AP|Greninja}}. It was later freed from their control when [[Alva]]'s Mega Wave Crystal was destroyed, and it escaped.

In ''[[JN084|An Adventure of Mega Proportions!]]'', [[Goh]] caught a {{DL|List of Goh's Pokémon|Camerupt}} on [[Mega Island]].

====Minor appearances====

In ''[[DP171|Dawn of a Royal Day!]]'', a Coordinator's Camerupt competed in the {{to|Arrowroot}} {{pkmn|Contest}}.

==Trivia==
* Camerupt's evolutionary line can be seen as a parallel to {{p|Sharpedo}}'s family. Both of them are dual-type Pokémon, both lines have identical base stat totals per evolutionary stage, both of their final evolved forms are used by [[Team Magma]] Leader [[Maxie]] and [[Team Aqua]] Leader [[Archie]], respectively, and both of their final evolved forms can [[Mega Evolution|Mega Evolve]].
* Camerupt is the only non-{{type|Rock}} Pokémon that can have the ability {{a|Solid Rock}}.
* Mega Camerupt is tied with {{p|Slugma}}, {{p|Torkoal}}, and {{p|Litwick}} for the lowest base {{stat|Speed}} stat of all {{type|Fire}} Pokémon.
* Aside from {{p|Diancie}}, the [[Event Pokémon|event-exclusive]] [[Mythical Pokémon]], Camerupt is the only Pokémon capable of [[Mega Evolution]] in Generation VII that cannot be obtained natively in {{g|Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon}}. All others are either in the [[List of Pokémon by_Alola_Pokédex_number_in_Pokémon_Ultra_Sun_and_Ultra_Moon|Alola regional Pokédex]], are obtainable via [[QR Scanner#Island Scan|Island Scan]], or are obtainable in [[Ultra Space]].

===Origin===
Camerupt resembles a {{wp|Bactrian camel}} (camels with two humps) with {{wp|volcano}}es on its back instead of humps, though it has proportions closer to those of {{wp|cattle}}, {{wp|muskox}}en, or {{wp|yak}}s. The blue rings on the sides of Camerupt may be based on a {{wp|carnival game}} of hitting the target, what enrages it and makes it erupt, as presented in the {{aniseries|DP}} episode ''[[DP140|Historical Mystery Tour!]]''.

Mega Camerupt, in contrast, resembles a {{wp|dromedary}} camel (which has only one hump). Mega Camerupt's "skirt" of fur resembles that of male {{wp|wild yak}}s. The "M" on its forehead may represent [[Team Magma]]; the team leader, [[Maxie]], uses a Mega Camerupt in {{g|Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire}}.

====Name origin====
Camerupt may be a combination of ''{{wp|camel}}'' and ''erupt''.

Bakuuda may be a combination of 爆発 ''bakuhatsu'' (eruption) and 駱駝 ''rakuda'' (camel). It may also involve ''Bactrian camel''.
