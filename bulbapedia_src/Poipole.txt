'''Poipole''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ベベノム''' ''Bevenom'') is a {{type|Poison}} [[Ultra Beast]] [[Pokémon (species)|Pokémon]] introduced during [[Generation VII]], in [[Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Naganadel}} when [[level]]ed up while knowing {{m|Dragon Pulse}}.

It is code-named '''UB Adhesive''' (Japanese: '''{{tt|ＵＢ：ＳＴＩＣＫＹ|ユービー：スティッキー}}''' ''UB: Sticky'').

==Biology==
[[File:Poipole USUM concept art.jpg|thumb|left|250px|Concept art of Poipole<br>illustrated by [[James Turner]]]]
Poipole is a small, purple [[Ultra Beast]]. It has a bipedal build with a large head and relatively small limbs with no visible digits. On its head are three spikes: one in the front and one on each side. A single gray needle protrudes from the top of its head and there are additional needles in the spikes on the sides of its head. It has oval eyes with a single eyelash-like spike above each; its mouth and eyes give off a cyan glow. It has a long, tapering tail with a single spine in the middle. While most of its body is dark purple, it has several fuchsia markings: at the tip of each head-spike, on its chest, on top of its head, and on its face.

In the games, Poipole originates from [[Ultra Megalopolis]] in [[Ultra Space]], where it is popular as a [[first partner Pokémon]]. However, Poipole originates from [[Poipole's world|an unnamed world]] in {{aniseries|PTS}}. It is capable of understanding human speech after spending enough time with humans. Poipole's large head is filled with a glowing, adhesive poison, which it can fire and spray at its oponents from the needles on its head.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20190113221043/https://www.pokemon-sunmoon.com/ultra/en-us/gallery-detail-5.html Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon site | Poipole]</ref> It laughs wildly while doing so. As seen in the animated series, Poipole can also shoot a harmless multicolored substance as a way to paint, which can be also used to communicate with [[human]]s and {{OBP|Pokémon|species}}. It is shown to have the habit to stand upside down and spin rapidly as a sign of happiness and excitement. Like all Ultra Beasts, Poipole stores [[Z-Power]], the same energy possessed by [[Totem Pokémon]], within its body, which takes the form of a red, flaring aura<ref>[[Wicke]], {{g|Sun and Moon}}:''"When the UBs pass through the Ultra Wormhole, they are bathed in a great deal of this energy and likely store it within their bodies."''</ref>. In ''[[SM084|The Shape of Love to Come!]]'', it is revealed that {{pkmn2|wild}} Poipole live in large numbers in huge nests that resemble wasp or hornet nests alongside {{p|Naganadel}}, who serves as a leader.

===Major appearances===
====[[Ash's Poipole]]====
Poipole debuted in ''[[SM055|The Professors' New Adventure!]]'', where it was traversing an [[Ultra Wormhole]]. It reappeared in ''[[SM061|A Mission of Ultra Urgency!]]'' and ''[[SM067|Love at First Twirl!]]''; it was {{pkmn2|caught}} by {{Ash}} in the latter episode. It eventually left Ash's [[Party|team]] in ''[[SM090|Securing the Future!]]'' and returned to [[Poipole's world|its home]]. Poipole was revealed to have [[Evolution|evolved]] into a Naganadel by the time it reunited with Ash in ''[[SM140|Z-Move Showdown!]]''.

===={{DL|Ultra Beasts (anime)|Ultra Space Poipole|Poipole (anime)}}====
Multiple Poipole appeared in ''[[SM084|The Shape of Love to Come!]]'' during a dream seen by Ash's own Poipole. They reappeared in ''[[SM089|The Prism Between Light and Darkness!]]'', where they attacked the [[Ultra Guardians]] to defend themselves until Ash's Poipole explained things to them. They reappeared in ''[[SM090|Securing the Future!]]''.

===Minor appearances===
A Poipole appeared in a fantasy in ''[[SM079|Showering the World with Love!]]''.

==Trivia==
[[File:803Poipole-Shiny.png|thumb|200px|Promotional artwork of a {{Shiny}} Poipole illustrated by [[Hitoshi Ariga]]<ref name=ShinyPoipoleNaganadel>[https://twitter.com/ariga_megamix/status/968050525705916416 Tweet about the Shiny Poipole and Shiny Naganadel event art] (posted on February 26, 2018)</ref>]]
* Poipole is the only [[Ultra Beast]] that can [[Evolution|evolve]].
* Poipole has the lowest base stat total of all the Ultra Beasts.
* Poipole and its Evolution were created by [[James Turner]], as were their {{Shiny}} forms.<ref>[https://twitter.com/JamesTurner_42/status/932109846811250689 Twitter - James Turner confirming shiny origin]</ref>
* Poipole shares its {{pkmn|category}} name with its Evolution, {{p|Naganadel}}, as well as {{p|Nidoran♀}}, {{p|Nidorina}}, {{p|Nidoran♂}}, and {{p|Nidorino}}. They are all known as the Poison Pin Pokémon.

===Origin===
Poipole is based on a bee larva.<ref>[https://twitter.com/JamesTurner_42/status/1071402286411243521 Twitter - James Turner revealing design origin]</ref>{{Dead link}} Like its Evolution, its needle-like appendages resembles the plunger of a {{wp|syringe}}, with the head representing the barrel or tube and the venom representing the liquid inside.

====Name origin====
Poipole is a combination of ''poison'' and ''pole''.<ref>[https://twitter.com/JamesTurner_42/status/931788320752918529 Twitter - James Turner confirming name origin]</ref> It may also incorporate ''purple''; or ''tadpole'', as it is the infantile stage of Naganadel.

Bevenom is a combination of ''baby'' or ''bébé'' (French for baby) and ''venom''.<ref>[https://twitter.com/JamesTurner_42/status/931665326772645888 Twitter - James Turner revealing Japanese name origin]</ref>
