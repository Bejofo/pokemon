'''Patrat''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ミネズミ''' ''Minezumi'') is a {{type|Normal}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s into {{p|Watchog}} starting at [[level]] 20.

==Biology==
Patrat is a rodent-like {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} that resembles a {{wp|chipmunk}}. It is mostly brown with a tan stomach and face. It has a large pointed tooth that sticks out of its mouth. Patrat has a flat face, and its cheeks are very round. Patrat has cheek pouches that it uses to store food. Its ears are small and pointed. Its head has a black band that covers the eyes, which have red irises with a yellow ring and black pupils. Its feet are small and black with three toes. Its long tail has a scruffy white tip and is usually standing up straight. This tail is used for communication; its tail is raised when it sees an enemy. Patrat is very conscious and wary regarding its surroundings and is always on guard. The {{aniseries|BW}} episode ''[[BW018|Sewaddle and Burgh in Pinwheel Forest!]]'' depicts Patrat living in tunnels.

===Evolution===
Patrat evolves into {{p|Watchog}}.

====Major appearances====
Patrat debuted in ''[[BW001|In The Shadow of Zekrom!]]''. They reappeared in ''[[BW002|Enter Iris and Axew!]]''.

In ''[[BW024|Emolga the Irresistible!]]'', three male Patrat were gathering apples, but their harvest was stolen by a {{TP|Iris|gluttonous Emolga|Emolga}} who tricked them with {{m|Attract}}.

In ''[[XY035|The Forest Champion!]]'', a Patrat, a {{p|Sentret}}, and a {{p|Pachirisu}} were gathering food, only to be scared off by an {{p|Ursaring}}. They were then saved by the forest champion {{AP|Hawlucha}}.

In a flashback in ''[[XY088|A Trip Down Memory Train!]]'', a Patrat befriended [[Clemont's Bunnelby]] when it was still wild.

Three Patrat appeared in ''[[HZ042|Transform! Hero of the Seas, Palafin]]'', under the ownership of the [[Snap Boys]].

==Trivia==
[[File:Patrat Yellow Battle Chess.png|thumb|Patrat in Pokémon Battle Chess]]
* Patrat shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Sentret}} and {{rf|Hisuian}} {{p|Growlithe}}. They are all known as the Scout Pokémon.
* Patrat has the lowest base stat total of all {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].
* Patrat appears in {{DL|Pokémon Battle Chess|Pokémon Battle Chess BW Version}}. It acts like a pawn piece in chess, but can also move one square horizontally.
* Coincidentally, Patrat's name and etymology is the same in both the English and Czech versions. In the Czech language, ''pátrat'' is a verb that means "to search" or "to scout."

===Origin===
Patrat appears to be based on a {{wp|prairie dog}}, {{wp|chipmunk}}, or {{wp|meerkat}}. The way it always look out for danger seems to be based on {{wp|scout}}s and doing reconnaissance.

====Name origin====
Patrat may be a combination of ''patrol'' and ''rat'', as well as a play on ''{{wp|pack rat}}''.

Minezumi may be a combination of 見 ''mi'' (looking) and 鼠 ''nezumi'' (mouse).
