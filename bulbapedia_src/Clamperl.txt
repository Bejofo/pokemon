'''Clamperl''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''パールル''' ''Pearlulu'') is a {{type|Water}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Huntail}} when [[Trade Evolution|traded]] while holding a [[Deep Sea Tooth]] or into {{p|Gorebyss}} when traded while holding a [[Deep Sea Scale]].

==Biology==
Clamperl is an aquatic {{wp|bivalve}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} that makes its home on the {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Sea Pokémon|seafloor}}. Its bivalve shell is dark blue with a silver rim along the inner edges. The shell is used to clamp down on prey, as well as for protection. A fully-grown shell will have nicks and scratches all over. Inside of the shell is a fragile, light blue body composed of six spherical extensions with a pink, pearl-like head in the center, usually depicted with its eyes closed. There is a circular white patch on each cheek.

The pearls produced by this Pokémon amplify psychic power and can be blue in color, as seen in the {{aniseries|AG}} episode ''[[AG093|Clamperl of Wisdom]]''. {{p|Spoink}}'s pearls come from Clamperl. These pearls are also 10 times more valuable than pearls produced by {{p|Shellder}}. It is carnivorous, clamping down on prey without letting go until it stops moving. When it is close to [[Evolution]], Clamperl will become too big to fit inside its shell. Clamperl is the only Pokémon that gains extra effects by holding a [[Deep Sea Tooth]] or [[Deep Sea Scale]]. Clamperl have been observed being carried by {{p|Drifblim}}, its natural predator.

===Evolution===
Clamperl evolves into either {{p|Huntail}} or {{p|Gorebyss}}.

===Main series===
====Major appearances====
Clamperl debuted in ''[[AG093|Clamperl of Wisdom]]'' as a resident of [[Maisie Island]]. {{OBP|Spoink|recurring}} lost its pearl and took a blue pearl that was created by a Clamperl from [[Isaiah]]'s lab, thus causing conflict with the Bivalve Pokémon. {{TRT}} also wanted to get their hands on the blue pearl.

In ''[[AG095|The Evolutionary War!]]'', [[Nancy]] and {{OBP|Keith|AG095}} each had a Clamperl. It was revealed that {{pkmn|Trainer}}s who live on the [[A-B-C Islands]] have rivalries over which of Clamperl's evolved forms was stronger. Both Nancy and Keith's Clamperl evolved during the episode, with Nancy's becoming a {{p|Gorebyss}} and Keith's evolving into {{p|Huntail}}.

====Minor appearances====
In ''[[AG126|Saved by the Beldum]]'', a Trainer's Clamperl was at the [[Ever Grande Conference]].

A Clamperl appeared in the opening sequence of ''[[M08|Lucario and the Mystery of Mew]]''.

In ''[[AG174|New Plot, Odd Lot!]]'', a {{pkmn|Coordinator}}'s Clamperl competed in the [[Appeal|Performance Stage]] of the {{ci|Mulberry}} {{pkmn|Contest}}.

In ''[[PK24|Meloetta's Moonlight Serenade]]'', a Clamperl attended {{an|Meloetta}}'s concert.

In ''[[XY022|Going for the Gold!]]'', multiple Clamperl were living in the {{DL|Ambrette Town|Ambrette Aquarium}}.

A Clamperl appeared in ''[[JN063|On Land, In the Sea, and to the Future!]]''.

A Clamperl appeared in ''[[JN088|Looking Out for Number Two!]]''.

A Vendor's Clamperl appeared in ''[[HZ008|The Door That Never Opens]]''.

==Trivia==
* Clamperl shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Shellder}} and {{p|Cloyster}}. They are all known as the Bivalve Pokémon.
* Clamperl is the only Pokémon that evolves by trade holding one of two different items.
* Clamperl can learn {{m|Iron Defense}} from a Move Tutor, but its [[Evolution]]s, {{p|Huntail}} and {{p|Gorebyss}}, can't.
* Clamperl is one of two Pokémon to have two different species-specific Stat-enhancing items associated with it, the other being {{P|Ditto}}.

===Origin===
Clamperl appears to be based on an {{wp|oyster}} or a {{wp|giant clam}}. Clamperl's appearance and its evolved forms suggest that it could also be based on {{wp|roe}}, the eggs of {{wp|fish}} and other marine animals.

====Name origin====
Clamperl may be a combination of ''clamp'' or ''{{wp|clam}}'' and ''{{wp|pearl}}''.

Pearlulu may be a combination of ''pearl'' and possibly لؤلؤ ''lulu'' ({{wp|Arabic language|Arabic}} for pearls).
