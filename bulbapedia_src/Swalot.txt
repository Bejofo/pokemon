'''Swalot''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''マルノーム''' ''Marunoom'') is a {{type|Poison}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Gulpin}} starting at [[level]] 26.

==Biology==
Swalot is a tall, purple, amorphous {{OBP|Pokémon|species}}. It has red, beady eyes and yellow whiskers. A female Swalot has shorter whiskers than a male. It has puckered lips and can fit an automobile tire in its mouth. Its hands have three fingers. It has a single row of black rhombi running across its midsection, and it can expand and contract its body at will. 

Swalot is capable of swallowing anything whole and can digest anything except its own stomach lining. It covers prey and opponents with a powerful poison. It likes eating poisoned and poisonous things. It lives in {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Grassland Pokémon|grasslands and savannas}}. As mentioned in the [[Sleep style|Sleep Style Dex]], Swalot sometimes sleeps with its mouth wide open.<ref>[[Sleep style|Sleep Style Dex entry]]: "It sometimes falls asleep with its cavernous mouth wide open. Perhaps it's dreaming that it found a huge meal and is trying to swallow it whole."</ref> It is believed that Swalot is dreaming of a huge meal it is attempting to swallow whole.
===Evolution===
Swalot evolves from {{p|Gulpin}}.

==In animation==
[[File:Sailor Swalot.png|thumb|250px|Swalot {{aniseries|JN}}]]
===Major appearances===
Swalot debuted in ''[[AG122|Deceit and Assist]]'', under the ownership of {{OBP|Anthony|AG122}}. He had been {{pkmn|training}} it since it was a {{p|Gulpin}}. It was used to compete in the [[Hoenn Grand Festival]], during which it battled {{an|May}} and lost.

In ''[[AG127|From Brags to Riches]]'', [[Dominick]] used a Swalot and {{p|Tropius}} in a [[Double Battle]] against {{Ash}}, where they battled {{AP|Corphish}} and {{AP|Torkoal}} at the [[Ever Grande Conference]]. However, Corphish ultimately managed to defeat Swalot.

In ''[[DP066|Lost Leader Strategy!]]'', [[Reggie]] used a Swalot in his battle against Ash. It battled {{AP|Staravia}} and was able to defeat him.

===Minor appearances===
In ''[[DP033|All Dressed Up With Somewhere To Go!]]'', a Swalot competed in the [[Pokémon Dress-Up Contest]], being painted to look like a {{p|Grimer}}.

A {{pkmn|Trainer}}'s Swalot appeared in ''[[XY039|Summer of Discovery!]]''.

In ''[[JN047|Crowning the Chow Crusher!]]'', a {{tc|Sailor}}'s Swalot competed in the [[Pokémon Grand Eating Contest]].

==Trivia==
* Swalot is the only Pokémon with a base stat total of 467.
* The illustrator of [[Pokémon Adventures]], [[Satoshi Yamamoto]], uses Swalot to represent himself in the volume's comment boxes.

===Origin===
Swalot may be based on myriad organs or glands, such as a {{wp|stomach}}, a {{wp|gallbladder}}, or a single, giant poison gland. It may also be based upon a generic organism that uses {{wp|pseudopodia}}. Its general shape resembles a {{wp|slug}}. Alternatively, it may take inspiration from a trash bag.

Swalot may also draw inspiration from the {{wp|Nuppeppo}}, a yōkai in Japanese folklore depicted as a blob with vestigial appendages. Other possibilities include ''{{wp|Physarum polycephalum}}'', sometimes referred to as the predatory slime mold, a reference to the sci-film {{wp|The Blob}}; single-celled {{wp|amoeba}}; or the {{wp|predatory tunicate}}.

====Name origin====
Swalot may be a combination of ''swallow'' and ''a lot''.

Marunoom may be derived from 丸呑み ''marunomi'' (swallowing whole).
