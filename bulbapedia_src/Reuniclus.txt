'''Reuniclus''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ランクルス''' ''Lanculus'') is a {{type|Psychic}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Duosion}} starting at [[level]] 41. It is the final form of {{p|Solosis}}.

==Biology==
Reuniclus is a pale green {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} surrounded by a blob of translucent, green gelatinous substance. It has a large, round head with a thin stripe down the center. Its eyes are black and oval, while its mouth is red and triangular. Its body is small with stubby arms and legs. However, there are several round objects floating next to its arms. These objects increase in size and darken in color the further they are from the body and form arm-like appendages. The gel around these appendages forms three-fingered hands. Above its head, two rounded, triangular objects are suspended in the gel, giving the impression of horns or ears. The gel forms a stubby, round tail at the base of Reuniclus's body. 

Reuniclus controls its powerful arms using psychic powers. It can form a network with the brains of other Reuniclus by clasping hands with them. It is said that the power of Reuniclus is so great, it can crush boulders psychokinetically. Although capable of using its psychic powers in battle, Reuniclus prefers to use its large arms to pummel opponents. Drinking the liquid covering Reuniclus's body is said to grant wisdom; however, the liquid is extremely toxic.

===Evolution===
Reuniclus evolves from {{p|Duosion}} and is the final form of {{p|Solosis}}.

==In animation==
===Main series===
[[File:Geraldo Reuniclus.png|thumb|250px|Reuniclus in {{aniseries|BW}}]]
====Major appearances====
Reuniclus debuted in ''[[M14|White—Victini and Zekrom]]'', under the ownership of [[Damon]]. It was seen in the {{pkmn|movie}} as Damon's partner during his travels, and it later battled [[Ash's Pikachu]].

====Minor appearances====
In a flashback in ''White—Victini and Zekrom'' and ''Black—Victini and Reshiram'', multiple {{pkmn2|wild}} Reuniclus once lived at the now-relocated [[Sword of the Vale]].

Reuniclus made its animated series debut in ''[[BW090|Jostling for the Junior Cup!]]'' and ''[[BW091|Battling Authority Once Again!]]'', under the ownership of [[Geraldo]]. It was seen participating in the [[Pokémon World Tournament Junior Cup]], where it defeated [[Cassie]]'s {{p|Sawsbuck}} before losing to {{Ash}} and his {{AP|Boldore}}.

In ''[[BW105|Lost at the League!]]'', [[Virgil]]'s {{p|Glaceon}} defeated a {{pkmn|Trainer}}'s Reuniclus during the [[Vertress Conference]].

After taking the power of [[Arcades]], [[Fraud]] could transform into one of seventeen [[type]]s of Pokémon without the need of a [[Burst]] Heart. His {{type|Psychic}} form was that of a Reuniclus.

[[Damon]]'s Reuniclus appears in the manga adaptation of ''{{ma|White—Victini and Zekrom}}''. It assists Damon in powering up the [[Sword of the Vale]] and fighting off his opponents.

==Trivia==
* Reuniclus can be seen as a counterpart to {{p|Gothitelle}}. Both are [[Generation V]] {{type|Psychic}} Pokémon that are the final stage of a three-stage evolutionary line with first [[Evolution]] at level 32 and second at level 41. Reuniclus is [[Version-exclusive Pokémon|exclusive]] to Pokémon White, White 2, and Shield, while Gothitelle is exclusive to Pokémon Black, Black 2, and Sword. They both have the same base experience and catch rates, gain new moves at similar levels, and have base stat totals of 490.
* Reuniclus was designed by [[Keiko Moritsugu]].<ref>[https://imgur.com/a/cgf0g14 Archive of deleted tweet by Keiko Moritsugu]</ref>

===Origin===
Reuniclus and the rest of its evolutionary line seem to bear reference to {{wp|Cell (biology)|cells}}, with the green substance surrounding their bodies resembling {{wp|cytoplasm}} and the objects inside their bodies representing {{wp|organelle}}s. They may also represent the process of {{wp|mitosis}}, as hinted by their Pokémon categories and the fact the line down their head also resembles a {{wp|cleavage furrow}}. Reuniclus being able to form a network and get stronger when shaking hands with other Reuniclus may hint at their hands being based specifically on {{wp|neuron}}s and their {{wp|synapse}}s. 

Reuniclus may also make allusion to the {{wp|Embryonic development|embryonary stages}}, specifically when the embryo is fully developed to become a {{wp|fetus}}, while the green jelly may represent the {{wp|amniotic fluid}}. It may also be based on a {{wp|homunculus}}, hinting specifically at the {{wp|cortical homunculus|sensorimotor homunculi}} due to its large disproportionate body parts (namely its head, arms, and mouth). It may also be based partially on an {{wp|amoeba}}, with its arms resembling {{wp|Pseudopodia|pseudopods}}.

====Name origin====
Reuniclus may be a combination of ''reunite'' and ''homunculus'', ''nucleus'', or ''cluster''.

Lanculus may be a combination of 卵 ''ran'' ({{wp|egg cell}}), and ''nucleus'' or ''homunculus''.
