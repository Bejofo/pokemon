'''Dwebble''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''イシズマイ''' ''Ishizumai'') is a dual-type {{2t|Bug|Rock}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Crustle}} starting at [[level]] 34.

==Biology==
[[File:Dwebble no rock.png|thumb|left|250px|Dwebble without its rock]]
[[File:Pokécology Hippowdon.jpg|250px|thumb|right|Dwebble has a mutualistic relationship with Hippowdon, helping to remove any stones stuck inside it.]]
Dwebble is a small, orange, hermit crab-like {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with large pincers. It has dark, oval-shaped eyes supported by eye stalks. It has a hook-like tail that is concealed underneath the sectioned chunk of gray rock it uses as a shell. It ties with {{p|Minior}} and {{p|Carbink}} as the shortest {{type|Rock}}.

When finding a rock to use as its shell, Dwebble inspects the rock to see if it can be used, then it digs a hole in the rock's bottom. It can expel a corrosive spray from its mouth to help it carve such a hole, or to mend its shell. If the rock breaks, Dwebble won't calm down until it can find a replacement. Its search for suitable rocks makes it a natural enemy of {{p|Roggenrola}} and {{p|Rolycoly}}. If it can't find a livable rock, it moves into the ports of a {{p|Hippowdon}}, where it dislodges any stuck stones; in return, the Hippowdon looks after the homeless Dwebble.

===Evolution===
Dwebble evolves into {{p|Crustle}}.

==In animation==

===Major appearances===
====[[Cilan's Dwebble]]====
In ''[[BW011|A Home for Dwebble!]]'', {{an|Cilan}} {{pkmn2|caught}} Dwebble after helping it retrieve its shell from a trouble-making trio of Dwebble. Since then, Cilan has used Dwebble in various {{pkmn|battle}}s. In ''[[BW062|Crisis at Chargestone Cave!]]'', it evolved into a Crustle during a battle against {{TRT}}.

====Other====
Three Dwebble debuted in ''A Home for Dwebble!'', where they bullied another {{TP|Cilan|Dwebble}} and stole its shell.

In ''[[BW023|Battling For The Love of Bug-Types!]]'', [[Burgh]] used a Dwebble during his [[Gym]] battle against {{Ash}}. It defeated his {{AP|Tepig}} but then lost to his {{AP|Sewaddle}}.

In ''[[M16|Genesect and the Legend Awakened]]'', multiple Dwebble were living at [[Pokémon Hills]]. They took a particular liking to [[Cilan's Crustle]].

===Minor appearances===
Two Dwebble appeared in ''[[BW069|Climbing the Tower of Success!]]'', both under the ownership of a {{pkmn|Trainer}}.

A Trainer's Dwebble appeared in ''[[BW103|Curtain Up, Unova League!]]''.

A Dwebble appeared in ''[[M23|Secrets of the Jungle]]''.

A Dwebble appeared in ''[[HZ027|As Long as I'm with My Friends]]''.

[[Burgh]] owns a Dwebble, which he used in a [[Gym]] {{pkmn|battle}} against {{adv|Black}} in ''[[PS478|Big City Battles]]''.

A Dwebble liberated by [[Team Plasma]] appeared in ''[[PS547|The Final Battle]]''.

==Trivia==
* No other Pokémon has the same [[Egg Group]] combination as Dwebble and its evolved form.
* Dwebble was leaked internationally in the [[Black and White (song)|opening]] for [[S14|Pokémon: Black & White]].

===Origin===
Dwebble is based on a {{wp|hermit crab}}, which lives within a hollowed-out rock rather than a discarded {{wp|seashell}}.

====Name origin====
Dwebble may be a combination of ''dwell'' or ''dwelling'' and ''pebble''.

Ishizumai may be a combination of 石 ''ishi'' (stone) and 住まい ''sumai'' (dwelling).
