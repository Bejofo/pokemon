'''Corphish''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ヘイガニ''' ''Heigani'') is a {{type|Water}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s into {{p|Crawdaunt}} starting at [[level]] 30.

==Biology==
Corphish is a red-shelled, crustacean {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a short tail similar to a lobster. Its eyes are saucer-like with small pupils, and it has three spikes on top of its head. It possesses four pairs of limbs in total: two large pincers used for gripping prey, and three pairs of legs used for walking. The upper segments of its legs, the underside of its body, and lower halves of its pincers are cream-colored.

A hardy creature, Corphish is able to live in {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Water's-edge Pokémon|polluted water}} and eat nearly anything. Though not native to [[Alola]], Corphish set free by Trainers there have easily adapted and thrived in the region. {{p|Feebas}} and Corphish are the only Pokémon known to be able to live in stagnant ditches. Corphish and its evolved form {{p|Crawdaunt}} are the only known Pokémon that do not flee when a swarm of {{p|Basculin}} appears in the lake it lives in.

====Major appearances====
=====[[Ash's Corphish]]=====
In ''[[AG023|Gone Corphishin']]'', {{Ash}} battled and eventually {{pkmn2|caught}} a Corphish. Aside from his battling prowess, he was the main comic relief of Ash's [[party|team]] in {{aniseries|AG}}.

=====Other=====
Corphish debuted in ''[[AG021|Which Wurmple's Which?]]''. Ash hooked it on his [[fishing]] line and it subsequently attacked him and {{ashfr}}.

In ''[[Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time & Darkness]]'' and ''[[Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky - Beyond Time & Darkness]]'', a Corphish kicked Team Razor Wind out of their guild.

In ''[[DP112|Cheers on Castaways Isle!]]'', a Corphish stacked rocks around a glowing jewel that turned out to be a {{p|Deoxys}}.

In ''[[BW099|A Surface to Air Tag Battle Team!]]'', [[Soren and Rocko|Rocko]] caught a Corphish whose {{m|Bubble Beam}} became a vital component for the "Bubble Drill" combination that he configured.

==Trivia==
* Corphish is the only {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a [[catch rate]] of 205.
* Corphish can be found in the [[Hoenn]] [[region]], but its [[Pokédex entry|Pokédex entries]] claim it came from another region overseas. This would make it the first exotic species in the [[Pokémon world]].
* Corphish's tendency to say "Hey, hey!" in such games as [[Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time and Explorers of Darkness]] and [[PokéPark Wii: Pikachu's Adventure]] is a reference to its Japanese name "Heigani".

===Origin===
Based on its {{game|Ruby and Sapphire|s|Pokémon Ruby}} Pokédex entry, Corphish may be based on the {{wp|Procambarus clarkii|red swamp crayfish}} (native to the southern and southeastern {{wp|United States}} and also native to northern {{wp|Mexico}}), which, like Corphish, are invasive pests in many countries. Corphish's ability to learn the move {{m|Dragon Dance}} may come from the literal meaning of the word "lobster" in Chinese, which is 龙虾 ''Lóngxiā'' (dragon shrimp).

====Name origin====
Corphish may be a combination of ''{{wp|corps}}'' (branch of military servicemen) or ''{{wp|corporal}}'' and ''{{wp|crayfish}}''.

Heigani may be a combination of 兵 ''hei'' (soldier), 弊害 ''heigai'' (harmful effect), and 蝦蟹 ''ebigani'' (crayfish).
