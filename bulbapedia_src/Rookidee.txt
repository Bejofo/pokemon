'''Rookidee''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ココガラ''' ''Kokogara'') is a {{type|Flying}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation VIII]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Corvisquire}} starting at [[level]] 18, which evolves into {{p|Corviknight}} starting at level 38.

==Biology==
[[File:821Rookidee Dream.png|thumb|left|200px|Rookidee's wings]]
Rookidee is a small, birdlike {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} that is primarily dark blue, including its wings, tail, back and part of its head. The lower part of its body and chest is yellow, and it has a plume of black feathers that begins from under its wings to its face, from which a few tufts protrude over its head, giving it a mask-like appearance. It has red eyes with white markings under them, which helps Rookidee to intimidate fearful Pokémon. It has small gray legs and feet, each with two toes on the front and one on the back.

Rookidee are feisty and territorial Pokémon and will bravely fight against anybody, no matter how big and strong that opponent is. They are able to store some of their own energy after every battle, so they can grow a bit stronger even if they lose. Using their legs, they are able to jump nimbly from place to place to confuse Pokémon bigger than themselves. Female Rookidee are fussier than the males, and will relentlessly attack anyone who dirties its wings. Rookidee preys on {{p|Grubbin}}.

===Evolution===
Rookidee evolves into {{p|Corvisquire}}, which evolves into {{p|Corviknight}}.

==Trivia==
* Rookidee shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Pidgey}}, {{p|Spearow}}, and {{p|Natu}}. They are all known as the Tiny Bird Pokémon, though Natu has also been called the Little Bird Pokémon.
* Rookidee and its evolved form are the first non-[[Legendary Pokémon]] to be purely {{type|Flying}}.
* Rookidee is tied with Natu as the [[List of Pokémon by height|smallest]] Flying-type Pokémon.
* Rookidee and its evolved forms were designed by [[Hitoshi Ariga]].<ref>[https://twitter.com/ariga_megamix/status/1213844996975017989 Tweet by ありがひとし/ArigaHitoshi (@ariga_megamix).] Posted on January 5, 2020.</ref>

===Origin===
Rookidee's design appears to draw inspiration from birds in the {{wp|Tit (bird)|Paridae}} family, such as {{wp|chickadee}}s and tits; in the latter example's case, it may specifically be inspired by the {{wp|Eurasian blue tit}} and the {{wp|great tit}}, or the {{wp|Japanese tit}} for its {{Shiny}} coloration. While these birds are not a member of the {{wp|Corvidae|corvid}} family like the apparent inspirations for {{p|Corvisquire}} and {{p|Corviknight}}, they are in the same order, {{wp|Passerine|Passeriformes}}, and were once considered to be corvids. Its color pattern is similar to the [[wp:Hooded mountain tanager|hooded mountain tanager]].

====Name origin====
Rookidee may be a combination of ''{{wp|Rook (bird)|rook}}'', ''rookie'', ''kid'', and ''{{wp|chickadee}}''.

Kokogara may be a combination of 子 ''ko'' (child) and 小雀 ''kogara'' ({{wp|willow tit}}) or 烏 ''karasu'' (crow).
