'''Poochyena''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ポチエナ''' ''Pochiena'') is a {{type|Dark}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s into {{p|Mightyena}} starting at [[level]] 18.

==Biology==
Poochyena, as its name implies, is a quadrupedal {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} similar in appearance to {{wp|hyena}}s and canines. Its body is primarily gray with a black face and throat, paws, and belly. Its eyes are red with yellow sclerae, and it has a red nose. Its lower jaw has two pointed teeth sticking out. The fur at the base of its tail is rumpled and shaggy. Poochyena has gray paw pads on its three-toed paws.

Poochyena have a very keen sense of smell, allowing them to find and chase their prey with ease. It takes a bite at anything that moves and makes the hair on its tail bristle out to try to intimidate its foes. Poochyena's vigorous barking attempt at opponents embodies Poochyena's cowardly nature. Known for its tenacious nature, it chases after prey alongside the pack until the victim becomes exhausted. If its victim strikes back, then it may turn tail. It is omnivorous and will eat anything. This Pokémon lives in {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Grassland Pokémon|grasslands, savannas}}, and {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Forest Pokémon|forests}}.

===NPC appearances===
* Pokémon {{2v2|Ruby|Sapphire}}, {{pkmn|Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire}}: At the beginning of the game, a wild Poochyena attacks Professor Birch. The player must use their [[first partner Pokémon]] to defeat it.
* [[Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time and Explorers of Darkness]]: Three Poochyena form Team Poochy. They specialize in catching criminals. The brothers chase their target until they are tired and then finish them.

====Major appearances====
Multiple Poochyena debuted in ''[[AG001|Get the Show on the Road!]]''. They attacked [[Professor Birch]] when he got separated from {{Ash}}, similarly to what happened in {{game|Ruby and Sapphire|s}}.

In ''[[AG011|A Bite to Remember]]'', [[Max]] befriended a Poochyena belonging to {{OBP|Katrina|AG011}}. He eventually helped it [[Evolution|evolve]] and become a protector of the preserve. [[Evolution|Brock's demonstration]] also featured a Poochyena evolving into a Mightyena.

In ''[[HS05|The Blue Badge of Courage]]'', the [[Gym Badge thieves]] used a Poochyena and a {{p|Zigzagoon}} to battle [[Misty's Psyduck]] and [[Sakura's Espeon]], but both were defeated.

In ''[[AG071|A Six Pack Attack!]]'', a {{pkmn2|wild}} Poochyena assisted Professor Birch in finding {{an|Professor Oak}}, {{an|May}}, and [[Max]].

In ''[[AG073|Grass Hysteria!]]'', [[Old Man Obee]]'s three Poochyena tracked down Ash and May after they got lost in the [[Forbidden Forest]]. They then joined in the fight against {{TRT}}'s mecha.

In ''[[XY107|An Explosive Operation!]]'', a {{tc|Team Flare Grunt}} used a Poochyena during a [[Team Flare]] effort to capture [[Z2]].

In ''[[JN054|Sobble Spies a Stealthy Strategy!]]'', [[Jessie]] pulled a {{DL|Rocket Prize Master|Poochyena}} from the [[Rocket Prize Master]] to battle Ash and [[Goh]]. However, it was defeated by [[Jacqueline]]'s {{p|Inteleon}}. It reappeared in a flashback in ''[[JN062|Not Too Close for Comfort!]]''.

===Pokémon Adventures===
{{main|Nana}}
A Poochyena [[nickname]]d Nana is first seen in {{adv|Ruby}}'s possession, one of the three Pokémon given to him from his father, {{adv|Norman}}, when he was very young. Even five years before ''[[PS181|Creeping Past Cacnea]]'', Nana was shown to be a formidable battler, injuring a {{TP|Zinnia|Salamence}} teaming up with [[Kiki]] and [[Rara]]. While she still hadn't evolved, Ruby saw a few wild {{p|Mightyena}} that attacked {{prof|Birch}} and envied how splendid their fur looked. A few chapters down the line, Ruby would obtain his own Mightyena in [[Dewford Town]] when he met up with {{Steven}}, and Nana evolved together with Kiki, who touched the champion's [[Moon Stone]]. This new evolved form saw a furthering of Nana's abilities, allowing her to perfect her {{m|Hyper Beam}} attack.

==Trivia==
* Poochyena has the lowest base stat total of all {{type|Dark}} Pokémon.
* Poochyena and its evolved form share their {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Drednaw}}. They are all known as Bite Pokémon.

===Origin===
{{Bulbanews|On the Origin of Species: Poochyena and Mightyena}}
Poochyena appears to be based on both {{wp|hyena}}s and {{wp|Wolf|wolves}}. However, its small size, typically tenacious nature, and affinity for biting resemble {{wp|Tasmanian devil}}s.

====Name origin====
Poochyena may be a combination of ''pooch'' and ''{{wp|hyena}}''.

Pochiena may be a combination of ポチ ''Pochi'' (a popular name for pet dogs in Japan) and ''{{wp|hyena}}''.
