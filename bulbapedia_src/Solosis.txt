'''Solosis''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ユニラン''' ''Uniran'') is a {{type|Psychic}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Duosion}} starting at [[level]] 32, which evolves into {{p|Reuniclus}} starting at level 41.

==Biology==
Solosis is a round, green {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} surrounded by a darker green, gel-like substance. This liquid covering allows Solosis to live under any conditions, even in the vacuum of space, but when taking heavy blows, its fluid covering may leak away. Its inner body has dark, oval eyes and a red diamond-shaped mouth. On the upper left side of its head is a curly, yellow growth that resembles the endoplasmic reticulum of a cell. It uses psychic powers to communicate with telepathy, as well as to drive away potential threats.

===Evolution===
Solosis evolves into {{p|Duosion}}, which evolves into {{p|Reuniclus}}.

===Major appearances===
Solosis debuted in ''[[BW035|Where Did You Go, Audino?]]'', under the ownership of [[Doyle]]. It was used to help locate some missing {{p|Audino}}. Upon encountering Ash and {{ashfr}}, Doyle commanded it to use Psychic on them as he thought they were suspects. Later, it helped up by getting the group over a cliff so that they wouldn't lose sight of Audino. Solosis was then used to battle {{TRT}} by using {{m|Confuse Ray}} on {{TP|Jessie|Woobat}} and {{m|Night Shade}} on {{TP|James|Yamask}}.

Multiple Solosis appeared in [[M14|''White—Victini and Zekrom'' and ''Black—Victini and Reshiram'']], under the ownership of [[Damon]].

===Minor appearances===
In ''[[XY039|Summer of Discovery!]]'', ''[[XY041|Foggy Pokémon Orienteering!]]'', and ''[[XY042|Battling Into the Hall of Fame!]]'', a {{pkmn|Trainer}}'s Solosis attended the [[Pokémon Summer Camp]].

A Solosis appeared in ''[[XY092|Cloudy Fate, Bright Future!]]'', under the ownership of a {{tc|Psychic}}.

==Trivia==
* Solosis can be seen as a parallel to {{p|Gothita}}. Both are [[Generation V]] {{type|Psychic}} Pokémon evolving once at level 32 and then again at level 41. Solosis is [[Version-exclusive Pokémon|exclusive]] to Pokémon White, White 2, and Shield, while Gothita is exclusive to Pokémon Black, Black 2, and Sword. They both have the same base experience and catch rates, gain new moves at similar levels and have base stat totals of 290.

===Origin===
Solosis and the rest of its evolutionary line seem to bear reference to {{wp|Cell (biology)|cells}}, with the head representing the {{wp|Cell nucleus|nucleus}}, the green substance surrounding their bodies resembling {{wp|cytoplasm}} and the objects inside their bodies representing {{wp|organelle}}s. They may also represent the process of {{wp|mitosis}}, as hinted by their Pokémon categories and the fact the line down their head also resembles a {{wp|cleavage furrow}}. It may also make allusion to the {{wp|Embryonic development|embryonary stages}}, specifically the stage of {{wp|morula}}.

Solosis's ability to survive in extreme conditions, including the vacuum of space, may be inspired by {{wp|extremophile}}s or other highly resistant {{wp|micro-animals}} such as the {{wp|tardigrade}}.

====Name origin====
Solosis may be a combination of ''solo'' (singular) and ''{{wp|meiosis}}'' or ''{{wp|mitosis}}'' (cell division).

Uniran may be a combination of ''uni-'' (prefix meaning ''one'', as in ''{{wp|unicellular organism}}'') and 卵 ''ran'' ({{wp|egg cell}}). It may also refer to ''se unirán'' (Spanish for "they will join together"), as Solosis appears to be made of two parts.
