'''Regice''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''レジアイス''' ''Regice'') is an {{type|Ice}} [[Legendary Pokémon]] introduced in [[Generation III]].

It is not known to [[Evolution|evolve]] into or from any other Pokémon.

It is a member of the [[legendary giants]] along with {{p|Regirock}}, {{p|Registeel}}, {{p|Regieleki}}, and {{p|Regidrago}}.

==Biology==
Regice is a large, blue {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} composed of solid {{wp|Antarctic}} ice. Its body is composed of angular crystal-like shapes and its face has an unusual pattern consisting of seven yellow dots in a "+" formation. It has four spikes on its back and its two legs are conical and it balances its entire body on their points. There are three fingers on each of its pentagonal arms.

According to legend, Regice was created by the [[Legendary Pokémon]] {{p|Regigigas}} using ice. It used to be worshipped by [[Human|humans]], however, at some point, ancient people started fearing its power and sealed it away. In [[Hoenn]], it was sealed in the [[Island Cave]]; in [[Sinnoh]], it was sealed in the [[Iceberg Ruins (Sinnoh)|Iceberg Ruins]]; and in [[Galar]], it was sealed in the [[Iceberg Ruins (Galar)|Iceberg Ruins]]. Regice moves around by floating in the air. Regice cloaks itself in air that is −328&nbsp;°F (−200&nbsp;°C), and anything near Regice will become frozen solid. Its deep-frozen body can't be melted, even surviving when submerged in molten lava. It is made of ice from the ice age. It is said that has slept in a glacier for thousands of years.

==In animation==
===Main series===
[[File:Regice anime.png|thumb|250px|Regice in {{aniseries|JN}}]]
====Major appearances====
====={{DL|Legendary giants (M08)|Regice|Regice (M08)}}=====
Regice debuted alongside the other members of its [[Legendary giants|trio]] in ''[[M08|Lucario and the Mystery of Mew]]''. It was a guardian of the [[Tree of Beginning]]. It first tried to attack {{Ash}}, but was stopped by {{OBP|Lucario|M08}}. In the end, it and the other guardians realized Ash and {{ashfr}} were not a threat and continued protecting the Tree of Beginning.

====={{DL|Brandon's legendary giants|Regice|Brandon's Regice}}=====
Regice made its animated series debut in ''[[AG190|Pace - The Final Frontier!]]'' under the ownership of {{an|Brandon}}. He {{pkmn2|caught}} it at some point in-between the events of ''[[AG186|Overjoyed!]]'' and ''[[AG188|Pinch Healing!]]''. Brandon used it to battle {{AP|Pikachu}}, but lost after a tough match. Brandon brought Regice with him to Sinnoh in ''[[DP128|A Pyramiding Rage!]]'' and ''[[DP129|Pillars of Friendship!]]''.

=====Other=====
A Regice appeared in ''[[JN071|Leaping Toward the Dream!]]'', where Ash, [[Goh]], and [[Quillon]] encountered it on [[Mount Coronet]]. After Ash's Pikachu failed to beat it, Quillon easily defeated it with his {{p|Urshifu}}. Quillon promised to return for it in the future as part of a study regarding the origins of Pokémon. In ''[[JN113|Chasing to the Finish!]]'', he was revealed to have caught it.

====Minor appearances====
In the opening sequence of ''[[M10|The Rise of Darkrai]]'', a Regice, Regirock, and Registeel fired {{m|Hyper Beam}}s that combined into one.

In [[PK18]], a Regice met Pikachu and {{MTR}}'s groups at the other side of an ice bridge and sent them into a lake.

Regice debuted in ''[[PS252|The Beginning of the End with Kyogre & Groudon XIV]]'', when [[Glacia]] was attempting to open the [[Island Cave]]. Together, {{Steven}} and {{adv|Sapphire}} created a team consisting of four {{p|Beldum}} and Sapphire's {{DL|Sapphire (Adventures)|Relly|Relicanth}} and {{DL|Sapphire (Adventures)|Lorry|Wailord}}, thus opening up the chamber containing Regice, as well as the other two [[Legendary giants|giants]], {{p|Regirock}} and {{p|Registeel}}. Regice used {{m|Superpower}} to stop {{p|Groudon}} and {{p|Kyogre}} from spreading destruction. After the battle, it and the other two giants—left weak and powerless—traveled to the mountains, where they were later caught by [[Brandon]] shortly before the opening ceremony of the {{gdis|Battle Frontier|III}}.

Brandon used Regice in his battle with {{adv|Emerald}}. It defeated {{adv|Crystal}}'s [[Bonee|Cubone]], which Emerald had borrowed to use in the [[Battle Pyramid]]. However, Regice was subsequently defeated by [[Emerald's Sceptile]] and its {{m|Iron Tail}}.

In ''[[PASS36|Rrmbll!! Serious Tower Battle]]'', [[Hop]], [[Marnie]], and [[Bede]] were revealed to have caught a Regice at the [[Crown Tundra]] during one of their expeditions as part of [[Peony]]'s expedition team.

==Trivia==
* Regice has the lowest base Attack stat of all [[Legendary Pokémon]] that do not evolve.
* Regice has the highest base Special Defense stat of all {{type|Ice}} Pokémon.
* In the international versions of {{3v2|Ruby|Sapphire|Emerald}}, there is an unobtainable Regice Doll programmed into the game. It was unlockable with the Japan-exclusive ''Regi-Dolls Decoration set'' [[e-Reader]] card.
* Regice shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Avalugg}}. They are both known as the Iceberg Pokémon.
* Regice's base Attack and Special Attack, as well as Defense and Special Defense, are reverse of Regirock's.

===Origin===
Regice may be based on the {{wp|golem}}s of Hebrew legend. The legends cast them as servants of higher powers and are said to have writing on their heads. When the writing is removed, the creature would be weakened, or even killed in some legends. Regice's face that consists of seven dots may be based on {{wp|Braille alphabet}} which was necessary to use to encounter the [[legendary giants]] for the first time in [[Sealed Chamber]]. The geometrical shapes that make Regice's body resemble naturally occurring {{wp|crystal}}s.

The five legendary giants may be collectively themed after historical epochs, with Regice representing the {{wp|Pleistocene|Ice Age}}.

====Name origin====
Regice may be a combination of ''regis'' (Latin for royal) and ''ice''.
