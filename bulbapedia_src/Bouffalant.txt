'''Bouffalant''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''バッフロン''' ''Buffron'') is a {{type|Normal}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It is not known to [[Evolution|evolve]] into or from any other Pokémon.

==Biology==
Bouffalant is a bovine {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a large, dark {{wp|afro}} on its head. Two large, curving horns, each with two golden growths resembling rings, protrude from the afro. It has a shaggy brown hide that ends at its knees and does not cover its face. A large, gray nose sits at the end of its short, beige snout, and its yellow eyes are partly hidden by its hair. There are black tear markings directly below its eyes. It has a large hump on its back. Each of its beige legs has a single, gray hoof and its tail ends in a stubby tuft of fur. 

A territorial Pokémon, it will headbutt anyone that attempts to invade its territory. However, in the {{aniseries|BW}} episode ''[[BW066|Baffling the Bouffalant!]]'', it is shown not to attack those with an afro hairstyle. Protected by its afro, which absorbs damage, its headbutts are strong enough to derail a train and send it flying. It can also destroy a car with just a {{m|Headbutt}}. The more hair a Bouffalant has on its head, the higher positions it has in the herd. A herd of Bouffalant consists of 20 individuals. For unclear reasons, a Bouffalant loses its hair from its head should it ever betray its herd.

Bouffalant is the [[signature move|only known Pokémon]] capable of learning the move {{m|Head Charge}}.

==In animation==
[[File:Alder Bouffalant.png|thumb|250px|Bouffalant in {{aniseries|BW}}]]
===Major appearances===
In ''[[BW031|Ash and Trip's Third Battle!]]'', Trip remembered seeing [[Alder]]'s Bouffalant as a child, which inspired him to become a [[Pokémon Trainer]]. Bouffalant physically debuted in ''[[BW052|Ash Versus the Champion!]]'', where it faced {{AP|Pikachu}} in a battle with {{Ash}}. Alder fell asleep during the battle, resulting in no one winning. Alder later used Bouffalant in a battle against Trip and his {{TP|Trip|Serperior}}, and he was able to win.

In ''[[BW066|Baffling the Bouffalant!]]'', multiple Bouffalant attacked anyone in their territory who wasn't wearing an afro.

===Minor appearances===
In the prologue of ''[[M14|White—Victini and Zekrom]]'', multiple Bouffalant were startled by the arrival of a dust storm and began to stampede towards [[Luisa]]'s village. However, the nearby [[Damon]] summoned {{OBP|Reshiram|M14}} to easily destroy the dust storm, thus placating the Bouffalant in time. In ''[[M14|Black—Victini and Reshiram]]'', this flashback instead occurred midway through the movie and involved Damon using {{OBP|Zekrom|M14}} to destroy the dust storm and stop the stampeding Bouffalant.

In ''[[BW053|A Maractus Musical!]]'', a Bouffalant participated in the [[Pokémon Performance Competition]] alongside an {{p|Elgyem}}.

Multiple Bouffalant appeared in ''[[M15|Kyurem VS. The Sword of Justice]]''.

In ''[[BW102|Team Eevee and the Pokémon Rescue Squad!]]'', multiple Bouffalant were living in a ranch at [[Virgil]]'s house.

* Bouffalant was designed by [[Takao Unno]].<ref name=GFYouTube-GFIllust2>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8c1X6A2Tyh0 GAME FREAK Illustration Demonstration: Episode Two [James Turner x HarmoKnight<nowiki>]</nowiki> - YouTube]</ref>

===Origin===
Bouffalant is based on a {{wp|buffalo}}, particularly an {{wp|American bison}}. However, the horns more closely resemble those of the {{wp|African buffalo}} or ''{{wp|Bison latifrons}}''. The fur on its head is made to resemble an {{wp|afro}}, inspired by the shaggy hairs on top of bison heads.<ref name=GFYouTube-GFIllust2/> The golden rings on its horns may be inspired by {{wp|Nose ring (animal)|rings put on cattle}}.

====Name origin====
Bouffalant may be a combination of ''{{wp|bouffant}}'' (puffy, round hairstyle) and ''buffalo''.

Buffron may be a combination of ''buffalo'', ''afro'', and ''bison''.
