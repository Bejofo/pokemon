'''Cascoon''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''マユルド''' ''Mayuld'') is a {{type|Bug}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Wurmple}} with a corresponding hidden [[Personality value#Wurmple's Evolution|personality value]] starting at [[level]] 7, and evolves into {{p|Dustox}} starting at level 10. It is the parallel of {{p|Silcoon}}, Wurmple's other evolved form.

==Biology==
Cascoon is a round, cocoon-like Pokémon. It is covered in purple silk, except for two holes where its red eyes are visible. The soft silk hardens into a tough cocoon that is able to endure attacks, and Cascoon will not move even when it is attacked. However, it never forgets the pain it endures and seeks revenge upon Evolution.

It lives in {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Forest Pokémon|forests}}, where it hides underneath large leaves and inside the gaps of tree branches. Additionally, it camouflages itself by attaching dead leaves to its body. As its cells create energy for [[Evolution]], the inside of the cocoon becomes hot. Cracks start to form in its casing when Evolution is near. Cascoon's silk is said to be thin yet strong, with a luster and texture superior to the silk from its counterpart {{p|Silcoon}}. Cascoon's silk is used to make high-quality clothing.

===Evolution===
Cascoon evolves from {{p|Wurmple}} and evolves into {{p|Dustox}}.

===Main series===
====Major appearances====
=====[[Jessie's Cascoon]]=====
[[Jessie]]'s Wurmple evolved into a Cascoon in ''[[AG024|A Corphish out of Water]]'', which also marked the species' debut. However, Jessie thought Cascoon was a Silcoon. As a result, [[James]] and {{MTR}} were left worried about Jessie's rage when Cascoon evolved into Dustox. Fortunately, Jessie was ultimately not disappointed when Cascoon did indeed evolve into Dustox.

=====Other=====
In ''[[JN011|Best Friend...Worst Nightmare!]]'', [[Goh]]'s three Wurmple all evolved into {{DL|List of Goh's Pokémon|Dustox|Cascoon}}. They reappeared in ''[[JN015|A Snow Day for Searching!]]''. In ''[[JN023|Panic in the Park!]]'', they all evolved into Dustox. The Wurmple reappeared in a flashback in ''[[JN046|Getting More Than You Battled For!]]''.

====Minor appearances====
A {{pkmn|Coordinator}}'s Cascoon appeared in ''[[AG121|Hi Ho Silver Wind!]]''.

In ''[[JN007|Serving Up the Flute Cup!]]'', [[Goh]] fantasized about a Cascoon while revealing the three Wurmple he {{pkmn2|caught}} could evolve into them.

In ''[[JN033|Trade, Borrow, and Steal!]]'', a Cascoon was part of [[Kricketina Kylie]]'s Bug-type Pokémon collection, as shown in her [[Rotom Phone]].

===Pokémon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire Animated Trailer===
In the [[Pokémon Omega Ruby and Pokémon Alpha Sapphire Animated Trailer]], a Cascoon surrounded {{ga|May}} and her {{p|Torchic}}, only for Torchic to scare it away with a {{type|Fire}} attack.

==Trivia==
* Cascoon shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Metapod}}, {{p|Kakuna}}, and {{p|Silcoon}}. They are all known as the Cocoon Pokémon.
** They also share the same [[Base stats|base stat]] total, learnset, [[Ability]], and EV yield.
*** This ties them as the evolved Pokémon with the lowest base stat total.
* Cascoon and Silcoon have exactly the same type, base stats, moveset, and shape, making them nearly identical.
* Cascoon is the only {{type|Bug}} cocoon-based Pokémon whose Defense stat does not decrease upon [[Evolution]].
* Cascoon is the only Pokémon found in a [[Friend Safari]] that is not its own type; it is found in the {{type|Poison}} Friend Safari even though Cascoon is pure Bug. However, it evolves into the Poison-type {{p|Dustox}}.
* The [[Pokéstar Studios opponents#UFO|UFO and UFO 2]] from [[Pokéstar Studios]] look similar to Cascoon.

===Design variations===
[[File:Cascoon Design Differences.png|thumb|Cascoon's design differences in {{aniseries|PTS}}]]
Cascoon, along with {{p|Silcoon}}, is sometimes depicted without the limb protrusions on its body in the {{pkmn|animated series}}, all [[core series]] {{pkmn|games}} since {{g|X and Y}} onward, and occasionally in {{game|HeartGold and SoulSilver|s}}. However, [[James]]'s Pokémon trivia cards showed that Silcoon and Cascoon indeed have those limbs. However, it was revealed in {{g|HOME|s}}, both Cascoon and Silcoon actually pops out of their limbs.

===Origin===
Cascoon seems to be based on a {{wp|moth}} {{wp|Pupa#Cocoon|cocoon}}.

====Name origin====
Cascoon may be a combination of ''cask'' (a container), ''cast'' (a shell used to immobilize body parts), ''cáscara'' (Spanish for shell), and ''{{wp|Pupa#Cocoon|cocoon}}''.

Mayuld may be a combination of 繭 ''mayu'' (cocoon) and ''shield''.
