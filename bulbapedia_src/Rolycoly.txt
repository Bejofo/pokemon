'''Rolycoly''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''タンドン''' ''Tandon'') is a {{type|Rock}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation VIII]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Carkol}} starting at [[level]] 18, which evolves into {{p|Coalossal}} starting at level 34.

==Biology==
Rolycoly is a {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} which resembles a small piece of coal. It has a single orange-red eye that can light up and illuminate dark areas,<ref name=SwShsite/> while the rest of its body is black. It has a rugged exterior and lacks any limbs. Its lower body has a wheel-like leg, which it rolls on and uses for movement, in a similar fashion to a unicycle.

Rolycoly was first discovered around 400 years ago in coal mines. It races around on grassy plains and the rough, rocky terrain in coal mines and caves, burning through the coal that sustains it. Rolycoly's diet also includes grass that gets tangled in its leg. Until about 100 years ago, every household in [[Galar]] had a Rolycoly, using the coal that it drops for cooking and heating up homes. Rolycoly's popularity continues even into the modern-day, as it is used for outdoor activities.<ref name=SwShsite>[https://swordshield.pokemon.com/en-us/pokemon-galar-region/rolycoly/ Rolycoly | Official Website | Pokémon Sword and Pokémon Shield]</ref> {{p|Dwebble}} is Rolycoly's natural enemy, as the former tends to think that Rolycoly is an inanimate rock to turn into a home.

Rolycoly and its evolved forms are the [[Signature Ability|only known Pokémon]] that can have {{a|Steam Engine}} as an [[Ability]].

===Evolution===
Rolycoly evolves into {{p|Carkol}}, which evolves into {{p|Coalossal}}.

==Trivia==
* Rolycoly and its evolved forms have several unique distinctions:
** Rolycoly is the only Pokémon that is not {{type|Fire}} to have access to the [[Ability]] {{a|Flash Fire}}.
** They are the only {{DL|List of Pokémon by color|black}} Rock-type Pokémon.
* Rolycoly and its evolved forms share their {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Torkoal}}. They are all known as the Coal Pokémon.
* Rolycoly has the lowest base stat total of all {{type|Rock}} Pokémon.

===Origin===
Rolycoly may be based on {{wp|coal}} and a {{wp|wheel}}. Its {{Shiny}} variant's gray coloration may reference how coal turns white with ash as it burns.

====Name origin====
Rolycoly may be a combination of ''roly-poly'' and ''coal''. It may also be a pun on ''{{wp|rolling coal}}''.

Tandon may be a combination of 炭 ''tan'' (coal) and どん ''don'' (bang).
