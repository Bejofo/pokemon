'''Silcoon''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''カラサリス''' ''Karasalis'') is a {{type|Bug}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Wurmple}} with a corresponding hidden [[Personality value#Wurmple's Evolution|personality value]] starting at [[level]] 7, and evolves into {{p|Beautifly}} starting at level 10. It is the parallel of {{p|Cascoon}}, Wurmple's other evolved form.

==Biology==
Silcoon is a cocoon-like {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} that uses silk to attach itself to tree branches. It is almost completely covered in white silk with only two small holes. A pair of red eyes can be seen through the holes, but they are partially hidden by a rough fringe of silk. The silk forms a hard cocoon that it can use to repel enemy attacks. Dew and rainwater collect on the silk, which provides Silcoon with nourishment.

Silcoon lives in {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Forest Pokémon|forests}}, where it conserves energy by moving as little as possible. This energy, which it had gathered as a {{p|Wurmple}}, serves as fuel for its further Evolution. Due to its cocoon, Silcoon cannot extend its limbs and can only move really slowly. However, its eyes allow it to be on the lookout for danger.

===Evolution===
Silcoon evolves from {{p|Wurmple}} and evolves into {{p|Beautifly}}.

==Trivia==
* Silcoon shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Metapod}}, {{p|Kakuna}}, and {{p|Cascoon}}. They are all known as the Cocoon Pokémon.
** They also share the same [[Base stats|base stat]] total, learnset, Ability, and EV yield.
*** This ties them as the evolved Pokémon with the lowest base stat total.
* Silcoon and Cascoon have exactly the same type, base stats, moveset, and shape, making them nearly identical.

===Design variations===
Silcoon, along with Cascoon, is depicted without the limb protrusions on its body in the {{pkmn|animated series}}, all [[core series]] {{pkmn|games}} since {{g|X and Y}}, and occasionally in {{game|HeartGold and SoulSilver|s}}. In {{aniseries|PTS}}, [[James]]'s Pokémon trivia cards showed that Silcoon and Cascoon indeed have those limbs. However, it was revealed in {{g|HOME|s}}, both Silcoon and Cascoon actually pops out of their limbs.

===Origin===
Silcoon seems to be based on a {{wp|Bombyx mori|silkworm}} {{wp|Pupa#Cocoon|cocoon}} due to its white color.

====Name origin====
Silcoon may be a combination of ''{{wp|silk}}'' and ''cocoon''.

Karasalis may be a combination of 殻 ''kara'' (shell) and ''{{wp|chrysalis}}''.
