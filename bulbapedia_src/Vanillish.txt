'''Vanillish''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''バニリッチ''' ''Vanirich'') is an {{type|Ice}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Vanillite}} starting at [[level]] 35 and evolves into {{p|Vanilluxe}} starting at level 47.

==Biology==
[[File:Snowless Vanillish.png|thumb|left|200px|A Vanillish with its snow covering melted]]
Vanillish is a {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a large, white, rounded upper body with swirled snow on top, overall resembling a scoop of vanilla ice cream in a waffle cone. Its eyes are dark blue with light blue sclerae and small ice crystals at the sides. Vanillish has a dark blue mouth, two ice crystals below its mouth, and stubby limbs that resemble icicles. It has a pointed lower body that resembles a large icicle.

Vanillish lives in southern, snowy mountains, which it migrated to during an ancient ice age. It creates ice inside its body by drinking water and then expels it to hide itself or freeze opponents, whose lives it spares after freezing because it is kind-natured. Its icy breath can reach temperatures of −148&nbsp;°F (−100&nbsp;°C). It can shatter frozen opponents with powerful headbutts. Vanillish are smaller in hotter regions and elusive on warm, sunny days, and although it is possible to refreeze its body despite melting, this process can leave its appearance warped.

==In animation==

===Major appearances===
Vanillish debuted in ''[[BW057|The Mighty Accelguard to the Rescue!]]'', under the ownership of [[Dr. Ferrara|Dr. Ferrara/Cryogonal Man]]. It battled [[Charles]]'s {{p|Accelgor}}, only to be [[Fainting|knocked out]] by its {{m|Swift}}.

In ''[[BW079|Caution: Icy Battle Conditions!]]'', [[Brycen]] used a Vanillish in a [[Gym]] {{pkmn|battle}} against {{Ash}}. It first went up against {{AP|Krokorok}}, who was eventually recalled for {{AP|Scraggy}}, who in turn defeated Vanillish with a combination of {{m|Focus Blast}} and {{m|Headbutt}}.

In ''[[XY057|Thawing an Icy Panic!]]'', a Vanillish and Vanilluxe were looking for a Vanillite who had gone missing. They created a big snowstorm in [[Coumarine City]] in the process of the search.

===Minor appearances===
A Vanillish appeared in ''[[XY080|Performing with Fiery Charm!]]'', under the ownership of a {{pkmn|Performer}}. In ''[[XY112|Master Class is in Session!]]'', it was used in the Master Class Pokémon Showcase in [[Gloire City]]. In ''[[XY113|Performing a Pathway to the Future!]]'', Vanillish was seen watching the same Showcase.

* Vanillish and its pre-evolved form, {{p|Vanillite}}, are the only {{type|Ice}} {{DL|List of Pokémon by shape|Pokémon consisting of a head and a base}}.

===Origin===
Vanillish is based on a {{wp|soft serve}} {{wp|ice cream}} served in an {{wp|ice cream cone}}, with snow for the scoop and ice for the waffle cone.<ref name=ND205>''Nintendo Dream'' Vol. 205, May 2011 ([http://web.archive.org/web/20110402003550/http://shellspider.blog101.fc2.com/blog-entry-562.html Shellspider's blog (Japanese)], [http://lavacutcontent.com/ken-sugimori-nintendo-dream/ Translation by Dr. Lava])</ref> The 'cone' itself also resembles an {{wp|icicle}}.

====Name origin====
Vanillish may be a combination of ''{{wp|vanilla}}'' and ''delish'' (slang term for delicious).

Vanirich may be a combination of ''vanilla'' and ''rich''.
