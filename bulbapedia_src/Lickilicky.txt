'''Lickilicky''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ベロベルト''' ''Berobelt'') is a {{type|Normal}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation IV]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s from {{p|Lickitung}} when [[level]]ed up while knowing {{m|Rollout}}.

(Specifics may differ in past games. Refer to [[#Evolution data|Game data&rarr;Evolution data]] for these details.)

==Biology==
Lickilicky is a dark pink, rotund {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a small tail and a long tongue. It has a yellow line pattern on its belly and a white bib-like pattern. It also has what appears to be a curled appendage on its head.

It has a big appetite. It can lick anything or anyone close by for any leftover crumbs. Its saliva contains a numbing solvent and can decompose anything, and it wraps its long tongue around things, like its prey, to coat them with its sticky saliva. It has space in its throat to store saliva, and it can also roll up its tongue and store it there. Lickilicky lives in forests with a large body of water. It uses its tongue with more skill than it would its hands and feet, and a current record of its stretch length exceeds 82 feet. Its tongue can also function with the same dexterity as a human hand. However, Lickilicky is rather clumsy with its own fingers.

===Evolution===
Lickilicky evolves from {{p|Lickitung}}.

===Major appearances===
Lickilicky debuted in ''[[M10|The Rise of Darkrai]]'', under the ownership of [[Baron Alberto]]. It was his main battling Pokémon, but it was incapacitated for a portion of the {{pkmn|movie}} due to {{OBP|Darkrai|M10}}'s {{m|Dark Void}}. During that time, Alberto briefly [[Human–Pokémon transformation|turned into a Lickilicky]], earning him the temporary pun moniker "Baron Lickilicky".

Lickilicky made its animated series debut in [[DP098|''If the Scarf Fits,'' Wear ''It!'']], where it evolved from a {{p|Lickitung}} that was left in the {{pkmn|Day Care}} that [[Angie's parents]] owned.

In ''[[DP185|Working on a Right Move!]]'', [[Conway]] used a Lickilicky during his {{pkmn|battle}} with {{Ash}} at the [[Lily of the Valley Conference]]. It almost defeated {{AP|Gible}} after {{status|confusion|confusing}} it with {{m|Lick}}. However, Ash switched Gible out for {{AP|Noctowl}}, which then defeated Lickilicky with {{m|Extrasensory}}.

===Minor appearances===
A Lickilicky appeared in ''[[DP126|Classroom Training!]]'', under the ownership of the {{ci|Snowpoint}} {{pkmn|Trainers' School}}.

In ''[[DP162|Playing the Performance Encore!]]'', a {{pkmn|Coordinator}}'s Lickilicky competed in the [[Contest Battle|Battle Stage]] of the {{to|Daybreak}} {{pkmn|Contest}} along with a {{p|Weavile}}.

In ''[[DP174|Last Call — First Round!]]'', a Coordinator's Lickilicky competed in a [[Double Performance]] during the [[Sinnoh Grand Festival]] alongside a {{p|Rattata}}.

In ''[[DP182|An Old Family Blend!]]'', a {{pkmn|Trainer}}'s Lickilicky competed in the Lily of the Valley Conference.

In ''[[JN103|The Future is Now, Thanks to Strategy!]]'', a Trainer's Lickilicky was used to battle {{an|Clemont}} at the [[Lumiose Gym]], only to be defeated by {{TP|Clemont|Luxray}}.

==Trivia==
* Lickilicky would have been obtainable in both [[Generation II]] and [[Generation III]] if it had existed, as in both there was a possible way to get a {{p|Lickitung}} that had {{m|Rollout}}. It shares this trait with {{p|Mamoswine}}.
* Despite having a clearly visible tail, Lickilicky belongs to [[List of Pokémon by shape|a group of Pokémon]] which lack tails. This is not the case for its pre-Evolution, {{p|Lickitung}}.
* Lickilicky's Japanese name is romanized as ''Beroberuto'' in ''[[M10|The Rise of Darkrai]]''.

===Origin===
Like its pre-evolved form, Lickilicky may draw inspiration from {{wp|Plethodontidae|lungless salamanders}}, some of which have projectile tongues and may secrete a sticky, irritating substance. It could also be based on lizards with long extending or projectile tongues like {{wp|chameleon}}s, as well as the ''{{wp|Akaname}}''. Considering the white bib-like pattern on its torso as well as its voracious appetite, Lickilicky may be based on an exaggerated concept of a bloated {{wp|gourmand}}, perhaps dressed in {{wp|Macaroni (fashion)|macaroni}}-style or {{wp|dandy}}-style fashion.

====Name origin====
Lickilicky may be derived from a repetition of ''lick'' or ''licky''.

Berobelt may be a combination of べろ ''bero'' (colloquial term for tongue), ベロベロ ''berobero'' ({{wp|onomatopoeia}} for licking), and べろっと ''berotto'' (licking or sticking out one's tongue).
