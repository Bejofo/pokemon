'''Floatzel''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''フローゼル''' ''Floazel'') is a {{type|Water}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation IV]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s from {{p|Buizel}} starting at [[level]] 26.

==Biology==
Floatzel is an orange {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} resembling an anthropomorphic otter or weasel with longer arms than legs. It has four fingers on each hand and three toes on each foot. Its feet are small in comparison to the rest of the body. It has two pointed blue fins on its arms; two aligned, backward-swept, pointed fins on its head; and two tails, which can spin like a propeller in a similar way to its pre-Evolution's. It has cream colorations on its forehead, eyelids, muzzle, belly, and the tips of its forelimbs and tails. It also has one cream, slightly protruding, oval-shaped spot on its back if it is female, and two of these spots if it is male. There is an orange teardrop shape near the bottom of its belly, and a pattern of two black triangles on the edges of its muzzle. It has small, sharp canine teeth, the top two of which can be seen when its mouth is closed.

Floatzel has a yellow flotation sac, which seems to behave similar to rubber. The flotation sac goes in a kind of ring behind its head, down in front of its arms, then back behind its rear above the tails. Floatzel's sac developed as a result of chasing prey in the water. To help it dive, Floatzel can deflate its flotation sac, and then inflate it once again when it wishes to surface. Floatzel's sac allows it to not drown, even in stormy seas. Along with hunting for food, Floatzel can use its swimming capabilities to assist in the rescues of drowning people, by carrying them like a raft. It is often seen near fishing hamlets.

Floatzel evolves from {{p|Buizel}}.

==In animation==
[[File:Captain Floatzel.png|thumb|250px|Floatzel in {{aniseries|HZ}}]]
===Main series===
====Major appearances====
Floatzel debuted in ''[[DP071|Pokémon Ranger and the Kidnapped Riolu! (Part 1)]]'', where [[Kellyn]] used his [[Capture Styler]] on it so he could use it to escape from a group of {{p|Sharpedo}} that belonged to [[J's henchmen]].

In ''[[DP083|A Crasher Course in Power!]]'', [[Crasher Wake]] used a male Floatzel during his [[Gym]] {{pkmn|battle}} against {{Ash}}. After battling hard against {{AP|Pikachu}}, Floatzel then went up against {{AP|Buizel}}, whom he lost to after a close-range {{m|Water Pulse}}. He knows many different defensive techniques to guard himself against attacks, such as inflating the flotation sac around his body and blocking with his fin. He reappeared briefly in flashbacks in ''[[DP178|For The Love Of Meowth]]'' and ''[[DP180|League Unleashed!]]''; and during the ending credits of ''[[M13|Zoroark: Master of Illusions]]'', where he was seen alongside his Trainer.

In ''[[DP174|Last Call — First Round!]]'', {{si|Kenny}} used a male Floatzel in the [[Appeal|Performance Stage]] of the [[Sinnoh Grand Festival]] along with {{TP|Kenny|Empoleon}}. Their [[Double Performance]] was going well until Empoleon's {{m|Flash Cannon}} sent Floatzel flying backwards. In ''[[DP180|Four Roads Diverged in a Pokémon Port!]]'', he and Kenny were given advice by [[Jasmine]] before being targeted by {{TRT}}.

====Minor appearances====
In ''[[DP091|One Team, Two Team, Red Team, Blue Team!]]'', the [[Pokémon Summer Academy]] lent a Floatzel to [[Conway]] for use in the second leg of the [[Pokémon Triathlon]].

In ''[[DP182|An Old Family Blend!]]'', a {{pkmn|Trainer}}'s Floatzel competed in the [[Lily of the Valley Conference]].

Two Floatzel appeared in ''[[M17|Diancie and the Cocoon of Destruction]]''.

A Trainer's Floatzel appeared in ''[[JN090|Showdown at the Gates of Warp!]]''.

A Floatzel appeared in ''[[HZ031|Song Within the Mist]]'', under the ownership of a {{OBP|Captain|HZ031}}.

===Pokémon Adventures===
In ''[[PS348|A Big Stink Over Stunky]]'', a Floatzel is first seen acting as the motor jet for [[Professor Rowan]] and his assistant ({{adv|Platinum}}'s [[Mr. Berlitz|father]]) as they arrived in [[Canalave City]].

A few volumes later, under Wake's command, {{adv|Pearl}} attempted to catch one himself, but failed to do so and resorted to catching the weaker {{p|Buizel}}.

==Trivia==

===Origin===
Floatzel may be based on a {{wp|weasel}} and {{wp|sea otter}} combined with various flotation devices such as {{wp|inflatable armbands}}, a {{wp|life jacket}}, and a {{wp|Lifeboat (shipboard)#Liferaft versus lifeboat|life raft}}. Floatzel's two tails can rotate like a screw, similar to how boats use {{wp|propeller}}s that push away water to move forward.

====Name origin====
Floatzel and Floazel may be combinations of ''float'' and ''{{wp|weasel}}'', as well as ''flotsam'' (property or debris from a shipwreck that float in the sea).
