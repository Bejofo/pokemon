'''Furfrou''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''トリミアン''' ''Trimmien'') is a {{type|Normal}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation VI]].

While it is not known to [[Evolution|evolve]] into or from any other Pokémon, Furfrou has ten different [[#Form data|forms]]. 

Furfrou can have its hair trimmed into nine different styles, changing its appearance from its '''Natural Form''' into one of the following trims, each named after the style its fur is trimmed and dyed in: '''Heart Trim''', '''Star Trim''', '''Diamond Trim''', '''Debutante Trim''', '''Matron Trim''', '''Dandy Trim''', '''La Reine Trim''', '''Kabuki Trim''', and '''Pharaoh Trim'''. Furfrou will revert into its Natural Form if five days have passed since it was trimmed. Other circumstances may also cause it to revert<!--Please do not expand here-->.

(Specifics may differ in past games. Refer to [[#Form data|Game data&rarr;Form data]] for these details.)

==Biology==
Furfrou is a quadrupedal, canine {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with thick, white fur that covers its black body apart from its paws and face. The fur is thicker and fluffier around its head and tail. It has round, red eyes with blue eyelids, a pointed snout with a round, light blue nose, and long rectangular ears. When its mouth is open, two pointed teeth can be seen on its upper jaw. 

Furfrou used to guard the king in the ancient times of [[Kalos]] and is known to be loyal to its {{pkmn|Trainer}}.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20160403204716/http://www.pokemonxy.com/en-us/pokemon/other_pokemon/#Furfrou Furfrou] (archived)</ref> Furfrou is said to be very intelligent.

Prior to [[Generation VII]], Furfrou was the [[Signature Ability|only known Pokémon]] that could have {{a|Fur Coat}} as an [[Ability]].

===Forms===
Furfrou has ten different forms named after the style its fur is trimmed and dyed in: Natural Form, Heart Trim, Star Trim, Diamond Trim, Debutante Trim, Matron Trim, Dandy Trim, La Reine Trim, Kabuki Trim, and Pharaoh Trim.

It is commonly seen with its fur trimmed and dyed into various styles. However, only those it trusts are allowed to cut its fur. In the past, aristocrats would even compete over who could trim its fur into the most exquisite style. Cutting its fur increases the swiftness of its movements.

===Form data===
Furfrou has ten different forms. It is in its Natural Form by default and can be groomed into one of nine different trims: Heart Trim, Star Trim, Diamond Trim, Debutante Trim, Matron Trim, Dandy Trim, La Reine Trim, Kabuki Trim, and Pharaoh Trim.

It will remain with one of these trims for five days after being groomed, after which it will revert back to its Natural Form. Furfrou will also lose its trim when it is deposited in a [[Pokémon Storage System|Box]] (in [[Generation VI]]), withdrawn from a Box (in [[Generation VII]]), deposited in {{g|Bank}}, or deposited in the [[Pokémon Day Care]] or [[Pokémon Nursery]]. However, it is possible to deposit trimmed Furfrou in {{g|HOME}} by [[transfer]]ing it from {{g|GO}} via the [[GO Transporter]].

The location at which Furfrou can be groomed differs between games:
*In {{g|X and Y}}, Furfrou can be groomed at {{DL|Vernal Avenue|Friseur Furfrou}} on [[Vernal Avenue]] in [[Lumiose City]] for {{PDollar}}500. The player gains access to more trims as they become more {{OBP|style|Lumiose City|stylish}}.
*In {{g|Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire}}, Furfrou can be groomed at the [[Pokémon Fan Club]] in [[Slateport City]] for {{PDollar}}500.
*In {{game4|SMUSUM}}, Furfrou can be groomed for {{PDollar}}500 by a {{tc|Punk Girl}} at [[Malie City]]'s salon or, in Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon, a {{tc|Beauty}} at [[Hau'oli City]]'s salon.

In Pokémon X and Y only, Furfrou's trims are not registered in the [[Pokédex]].

====Major appearances====
Multiple Furfrou appeared in ''[[XY008|Grooming Furfrou!]]'', including one that belonged to {{OBP|Jessica|XY008}}. It initially did not approve of her as its Pokémon Stylist and thus she had difficulties with it, but their relationship improved after {{TRT}} attempted to steal it. This episode featured Furfrou's Heart, Diamond, La Reine, and Kabuki Trims.

A Matron Trim Furfrou appeared in ''[[XY018|Awakening the Sleeping Giant!]]'', under the ownership of [[Princess Allie]]. It was used in a battle against {{Ash}}, during which it was shocked by {{AP|Pikachu}}'s {{m|Thunderbolt}}. Because its fur was ruined in the process, and she was not content with Furfrou battling without looking beautiful, Allie forfeited the match.

A Dandy Trim Furfrou appeared in ''[[XY047|Dreaming a Performer's Dream!]]'', under the ownership of [[Kayleigh]]. It competed with its {{pkmn|Trainer}} in a Rookie Class Pokémon Showcase. However, their performance was interrupted by a {{TP|Serena|Pancham}} that appeared onstage and began imitating Furfrou's moves. After Pancham broke a stage lamp and fell on the stage, it ran off the stage in fear.

A Furfrou appeared in ''[[XY060|A Showcase Debut!]]'', under the ownership of [[Alouette]]. It was briefly stylized into a Pharaoh Trim. It appeared again in ''[[XY112|Master Class is in Session!]]'', where it was used in the Master Class Pokémon Showcase in [[Gloire City]].

A Matron Trim Furfrou appeared in ''[[XY080|Performing with Fiery Charm!]]'', under the ownership of [[Delilah]]. It assisted the other judges in evaluating the baking during the Theme Performance of the [[Dendemille Town]] {{pkmn|Showcase}} Rookie Class.

A Kabuki Trim Furfrou appeared in ''[[XY125|A League of His Own!]]'', under the ownership of [[Everett]]. Everett used it in his battle against Ash, where it faced off against {{AP|Pikachu}} but was eventually defeated.

A Trainer's Heart Trim Furfrou appeared in ''[[SM099|We Know Where You're Going, Eevee!]]''. It encountered an [[Sandy|Eevee]], who tried to greet it, but it wasn't impressed by Eevee's look. It was then called away by its Trainer. It later appeared when Eevee and {{TP|Lana|Popplio}} were running away from an {{rf|Alolan}} {{p|Persian}}, and it was nearly hit by {{m|Aqua Jet}}.

Three Furfrou appeared in ''[[SM124|Living on the Cutting Edge!]]''. They were about to be trimmed by {{OBP|Yoko|SM124}} when a {{DL|Ultra Beasts (anime)|Kartana}} showed up and trimmed them instead. With their new trims, they resembled {{rf|Alolan}} {{p|Vulpix}} and {{p|Ninetales}}, and {{p|Arceus}}, respectively. Although Yoko was unhappy with their new trims, the Furfrou were quite pleased by how they looked.

==Trivia==
* Furfrou was [[n:New Pokémon spotted in Pokémon Direct|unintentionally revealed in a Pokémon Direct on September 4, 2013]].
* Furfrou is the only {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a [[catch rate]] of 160.
* Furfrou is currently the only non-[[Generation V]] Pokémon that does not appear in any of the [[core series]] [[Nintendo Switch]] games, though it is set to appear in the upcoming {{g|Legends: Z-A}}.

===Origin===
Furfrou appears to be based on a {{wp|poodle}}. Its Natural Form may draw some inspiration from {{wp|Afghan Hound}}s.

====Name origin====
Furfrou may be a combination of ''fur'' and ''frou-frou'' (fancy, overly elaborate).

Trimmien may be a combination of ''trimming'', ''chien'' (French for dog), and possibly a play on the phrase ''très bien'' (French for excellent).
