'''Zebstrika''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ゼブライカ''' ''Zebraika'') is an {{type|Electric}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Blitzle}} starting at [[level]] 27.

==Biology==
Zebstrika is a zebra-like {{OBP|Pokémon|species}}. Its coat is black with white, jagged stripes all over its body. There is one pointed stripe extending from its nose, two elaborate stripes on its neck and chest, one zigzag stripe each on its jaws, back, and hindquarters, one triangular stripe on each of its thighs, and two stripes banding each of its legs. Its ears are triangular with blue insides, while its eyes are blue with yellow sclerae and white eyelashes. Its nose is black, and its hooves are gray. A white, spiked mane runs the length of its body, beginning with two horn-like extensions on its forehead and running down its back to end in a long tail with a starburst-shaped tip. 

Zebstrika has an aggressive and volatile personality. This irritable Pokémon fires lightning bolts from its mane when angry. The sign of that is when its white mane illuminates in a yellow glow. Zebstrika can move as quickly as lightning, releasing thunderclaps at full gallop. Zebstrika is known to lead {{p|Blitzle}} herds into thunderstorms. The main reason is to use the lightning unleashed by the storm to charge up Zebstrika's herd.

===Evolution===
Zebstrika evolves from {{p|Blitzle}}.

==In animation==
===Main series===
[[File:Elesa Zebstrika.png|thumb|250px|Zebstrika in {{aniseries|BW}}]]
====Major appearances====
Zebstrika debuted in ''[[BW039|Reunion Battles In Nimbasa!]]'', under the ownership of [[Stephan]]. It was revealed to have evolved from his Blitzle. It reappeared in [[BW040|the next episode]], where it was used in a {{pkmn|battle}} against {{an|Bianca}} and her {{TP|Bianca|Minccino}} at the [[Club Battle]] tournament and won. Zebstrika reappeared again in ''[[BW106|Strong Strategy Steals the Show!]]'', where it battled {{Ash}} and his {{AP|Palpitoad}} at the [[Vertress Conference]], with the battle resulting in a tie.

In ''[[BW049|Enter Elesa, Electrifying Gym Leader!]]'', [[Elesa]]'s Zebstrika defeated Bianca's {{p|Shelmet}}, Minccino, and {{TP|Bianca|Pignite}} during a [[Gym]] battle. In ''[[BW050|Dazzling the Nimbasa Gym!]]'', it was used during Elesa's Gym battle against Ash; it battled Ash's Palpitoad and lost.

====Minor appearances====
Multiple Zebstrika appeared in the opening sequence of ''[[M14|Black—Victini and Reshiram]]''.

A {{pkmn|Trainer}}'s Zebstrika appeared in ''[[BW067|Cilan Takes Flight!]]''.

==Trivia==
* Zebstrika shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Electivire}}. They are both known as the Thunderbolt Pokémon.
* Zebstrika is the only Pokémon with a base stat total of 497.
* All of Zebstrika's Abilities make it immune to a specific type. Excluding Pokémon with only one possible Ability, it shares this distinction with its pre-evolved form, Blitzle.

===Origin===
Zebstrika appears to be based on a {{wp|zebra}}. It may also be based on two of {{wp|Helios}}'s {{wp|Helios#Horses of Helios|steeds that pulled his chariot}}—Bronte and Sterope—which are Greek for thunder and lightning, respectively, as Zebstrika's species in Japanese can be translated as the "Thunder and Lightning Pokémon".

====Name origin====
Zebstrika may be a combination of ''{{wp|zebra}}'' and ''lightning strike''.

Zebraika may be a combination of ''zebra'' and 雷火 ''raika'' (flash of lightning).
