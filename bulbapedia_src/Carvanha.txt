'''Carvanha''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''キバニア''' ''Kibanha'') is a dual-type {{2t|Water|Dark}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s into {{p|Sharpedo}} starting at [[level]] 30.

==Biology==
Carvanha is a fish {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a body covered in tiny, sharp {{wp|denticle}}s. The top half of its spherical body is blue, while the bottom half is red with a yellow, four-pointed star below its mouth. It has two yellow {{wp|dorsal fins}} above its eyes that resemble shark fins, and a matching {{wp|Fish anatomy#fins|pelvic fin}} on its belly. Its pectoral fins are also yellow. The caudal fin is composed of two red spines with a thin yellow membrane stretched between them.

It has powerful jaws and razor-sharp teeth that are able to chew through solid steel, rip up boat hulls, and rip wooden boats to pieces. As a result, sailors avoid getting their boats dented by it at all costs. Carvanha has been responsible for the destruction of many boats that come into contact with it. They mercilessly swarm any intruder and prey that invades their territory, but this behaviour belies their cowardly disposition when alone. It only attacks when in a group of five or more, becoming instantly vicious. Carvanha is drawn towards the scent of blood. It lives in {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Sea Pokémon|rivers that course through jungles}}, as well as temperate regions. Each Carvanha school has its own territory. It competes with {{p|Basculin}} for food.

Prior to [[Generation V]], Carvanha and its evolved form, {{p|Sharpedo}}, were the [[Signature Ability|only known Pokémon]] that could have {{a|Rough Skin}} as an [[Ability]].

==Trivia==
* Carvanha has the lowest base {{stat|Defense}} stat of all {{type|Dark}} Pokémon, and is tied with {{p|Feebas}} and {{form|Wishiwashi|Solo Form}} {{p|Wishiwashi}} for the lowest base Defense stat of all {{type|Water}} Pokémon.
** Carvanha is also tied with {{p|Caterpie}}, {{p|Weedle}}, {{p|Magikarp}}, {{p|Igglybuff}}, {{form|Deoxys|Attack Forme}} {{p|Deoxys}}, and {{p|Stonjourner}} for the lowest base {{stat|Special Defense}} stat of all Pokémon.
* Carvanha's evolutionary line may be considered a counterpart of {{p|Numel}}'s evolutionary line. Both of them are dual-type Pokémon, both lines have identical base stat totals per evolutionary stage, both of their final Evolutions are used by [[Team Aqua]] Leader [[Archie]] and [[Team Magma]] Leader [[Maxie]], respectively, and both of their final Evolutions can [[Mega Evolution|Mega Evolve]].

===Origin===
Carvanha appears to be based on a {{wp|piranha}}, with its red underside particularly bringing to mind the {{wp|red-bellied piranha}}. The star on its underside may allude to sharks like {{wp|starspotted smooth-hound}} or {{wp|starry smooth-hound}}. Carvanha's aggressive behavior of attacking boat hulls to sink them, combined with its round body and star-like pattern resembling an impact explosion, suggests it may be inspired by cannonballs used in naval warfare.

====Name origin====
Carvanha may be a combination of ''carve'' or ''carnivore'' and ''piranha''. It may also involve ''carnage''.

Kibanha may be a combination of 牙 ''kiba'' (fang) and ''piranha''.
