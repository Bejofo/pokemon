'''Boltund''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''パルスワン''' ''Pulsewan'') is an {{type|Electric}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation VIII]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Yamper}} starting at [[level]] 25.

==Biology==
Boltund is a quadrupedal, canine {{OBP|Pokémon|species}}. Its two front paws are white with a yellow stripe on each leg above the white part, which leads to its desaturated green legs. Its two back paws are a darker yellow with a lighter yellow on top of it that also leads to desaturated green legs. It has a thin, yellow tail and hind legs with two spikes on the top of each of them. When it raises its tail, the tail takes the shape of a lightning bolt. The bottom half of its body is mainly desaturated green, while the upper part is mainly yellow. Boltund's head is yellow with white markings that extend over its muzzle, under its eyes, and in between its eyes, going up to its forehead. This white spot also goes down and ends in a triangle shape on the front of its chest. Its eyes are yellow, mint-green, white, and black with no distinct pupil. An outer ring of a light yellow is also around each eye. The inside of its ears is yellow with an outer ring of black. The desaturated green and yellow halves of its body connect on Boltund's shoulders, with the yellow part extending down in its front to cover Boltund's underbelly.

Boltund can channel the electricity that it generates to its legs in order to keep them strong, boosting its strength and allowing it to run nonstop for three full days. It can easily run faster than {{tt|50 mph|80 km/h}} at top speed. It serves as the enemy of {{p|Thievul}}.

==Trivia==
[[File:Company Icon DENSOKU.png|thumb|150px|Icon of DENSOKU]]
* Boltund is the only Pokémon capable of having {{a|Strong Jaw}} naturally as an Ability that cannot learn {{m|Ice Fang}}.
* Boltund shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Dachsbun}}. They are both known as the Dog Pokémon.
* The company logo for [[Poké Job|DENSOKU]] depicts a silhouette of Boltund.

===Origin===
Boltund appears to be based on a {{wp|greyhound}} and an {{wp|English Foxhound}}. It could also be based on the {{wp|lurcher}} or {{wp|longdog}} given its generic {{wp|sighthound}} appearance. Boltund's relationship with {{p|Thievul}} may be a reference to {{wp|fox hunting}}, where {{wp|red fox}}es were hunted by foxhounds.

====Name origin====
Boltund may be a combination of ''bolt'' and ''Hund'' (German and Swedish for dog) or ''hound''.

Pulsewan may be a combination of ''{{wp|Pulse (physics)|pulse}}'' and ワンワン ''wanwan'' (onomatopoeia for barking). It could also be a [[List of references to Pulseman|reference]] to {{wp|Pulseman}}, a video game developed by [[Game Freak]].
