'''Swellow''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''オオスバメ''' ''Ohsubame'') is a dual-type {{2t|Normal|Flying}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s from {{p|Taillow}} starting at [[level]] 22.

==Biology==
Swellow is a large, dark-blue {{wp|bird|avian}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a red breast and face. A crest of feathers sweeps back from the top of its head. A blue stripe separates the red markings on its head and chest, and it has a white underside. It has small, narrow eyes, a pointed, yellow beak, and red feet with black talons. Each foot has three forward-facing toes and one pointed backward. Two long, pointed feathers with red tips form its tail and are an indication of good health when standing up straight. Swellow's wings taper to a sharp point. An impressive dive-bomber, it will {{m|Aerial Ace|never miss its target}}. Additionally, it takes great pride in its glossy plumage and often cleans the wings of other Swellow. It searches for {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Grassland Pokémon|warm meadows}} while in the air. Swellow preys on {{p|Wurmple}}.

==Game data==
===NPC appearances===
* [[Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time and Explorers of Darkness]]: Swellow is one of the members of [[Team Tasty]], alongside {{p|Wurmple}}. Throughout the game, Wurmple is scared that Swellow will eat him. Only at the end of the game does Swellow realize this. He later reappears as a recruitable character in Super Mystery Dungeon, still proudly declaring himself as Wurmple's teammate.

==Trivia==

===Origin===
Swellow seems to be based on a {{wp|swallow}}, specifically the {{wp|barn swallow}} or {{wp|welcome swallow}}. Its colors and {{wp|Crest (feathers)|crest}} are also similar to those of a {{wp|treeswift}}. Its predatory behavior and speedy flight may be based on {{wp|falcon}}s or the {{wp|swallow-tailed kite}}. Its {{Shiny}} colors and long tail feathers resemble those of the {{wp|resplendent quetzal}}, and said Shiny coloration may have been inspired by the {{wp|violet-green swallow}}.

Its tail feathers resemble a pair of {{wp|katana}} swords. As the move {{m|Aerial Ace}} (Japanese: つばめがえし ''Swallow Return'') is based on the sword technique inspired by the motion of a swallow's tail in flight, "Turning Swallow Cut" by {{wp|Sasaki Kojirō}}.   

====Name origin====
Swellow may be a combination of ''swell'' (excellent, or to grow larger) and ''swallow''.

Ohsubame may be a corruption of 大燕 ''ōtsubame'' (large swallow).
