'''Tympole''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''オタマロ''' ''Otamaro'') is a {{type|Water}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Palpitoad}} starting at [[level]] 25, which evolves into {{p|Seismitoad}} starting at level 36.

==Biology==
Tympole is a round, tadpole-like {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a tan, circular face. It has large, oval, black eyes; a wide mouth with down-turned corners; and black eyebrows in the shape of quarter notes. Its spherical body is black with no visible limbs. Instead, it has two black-and-blue ringed cheeks akin to half-spheres on either side of its head. It has a long, blue tail with a fan-like shape and a black spot at its base.

Tympole vibrates its cheeks to create a sound too high-pitched for humans and other species of Pokémon to hear. The sounds are used in rhythm to communicate with others of its kind, particularly for warning of danger. They are known to sing in high-pitched voices, which creates graceful ripples on the water's surface. The {{aniseries|BW}} episode ''[[BW032|Facing Fear with Eyes Wide Open!]]'' reveals that Tympole swim and attack in groups.

===Evolution===
Tympole evolves into {{p|Palpitoad}}, which evolves into {{p|Seismitoad}}.

==Trivia==
* Tympole shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Poliwag}}, {{p|Poliwhirl}}, and {{p|Poliwrath}}. They are all known as the Tadpole Pokémon.
* Although it is simulated as swimming in [[Pokédex 3D]], Tympole does not display the bubble effect that all other Pokémon simulated in an aquatic environment, such as {{p|Eelektrik}} and {{p|Stunfisk}}, display. The reason for this is unknown.

===Origin===
Tympole appears to be a combination of a {{wp|toad}} {{wp|tadpole}} and a {{wp|loudspeaker}}. Its spherical bumps resemble {{wp|headphones}}, and were probably inspired by either the toads' "warts" (known as {{wp|parotoid gland}}s), or the {{wp|tympanum (anatomy)|tympanum}} of a real-life toad, an eardrum-like body part located in the same area which the toad uses to hear. Its eyebrows resemble {{wp|musical note}}s, specifically the {{wp|quarter note}}.

Tympole and Palpitoad's designs were worked backwards from Seismitoad's design.<ref name=ND204>''Nintendo Dream'' Vol. 204, April 2011 ([https://web.archive.org/web/20110704155314/http://shellspider.blog101.fc2.com/blog-entry-502.html Shellspider's blog (Japanese)], [http://lavacutcontent.com/ken-sugimori-nintendo-dream-6/ Translation by Dr. Lava])</ref>

====Name origin====
Tympole may be a combination of ''{{wp|timpani}}'' or ''{{wp|tympanum}}'' (both being types of drums), or ''tympanic membrane'' ({{wp|eardrum}}; referring to the bumps on each side of its head), and ''{{wp|tadpole}}''.

Otamaro may be a combination of お玉杓子 ''otamajakushi'' (tadpole; musical notes) and 麿 ''maro'' (affectionate suffix for young men) or 麻呂眉 ''maro-mayu'' (shaved eyebrows).
