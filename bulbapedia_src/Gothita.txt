'''Gothita''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ゴチム''' ''Gothimu'') is a {{type|Psychic}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Gothorita}} starting at [[level]] 32, which evolves into {{p|Gothitelle}} starting at level 41.

==Biology==
Gothita is a small, humanoid {{OBP|Pokémon|species}}. A small tuft of hair sticks up from the top of its purple head, and it has two white, bow-shaped feelers on each side of the head and a third bow-shaped feeler on its neck. These ribbon-like feelers are used to amplify Gothita's psychic power, and will cause Gothita to cry if touched. It has two large blue eyes with three eyelashes on each and small red lips. Its torso is black with a white zigzagging line running horizontally through the middle. Its arms and legs are short and stubby with no visible digits.

Gothita intently observes those around it, looking at something only it can see. It can become so focused that it ends up being unaware of attacks. Gothita are normally known to be innocent. It is usually quiet, but sometimes it makes muted cries, even when alone. This is believed to be Gothita communicating with invisible beings.

===Evolution===
Gothita evolves into {{p|Gothorita}}, which evolves into {{p|Gothitelle}}.

==In animation==
[[File:Nini Gothita.png|thumb|250px|Gothita in the {{aniseries|XY}}]]
===Major appearances===
Gothita debuted in ''[[BW035|Where Did You Go, Audino?]]'', under the ownership of [[Christie]]. It was used to help out on the disappearance of {{p|Audino}}. After two Audino destroyed a bridge with {{m|Psychic}} so the group could not follow them, Christie commanded Gothita to use Telekinesis to get them across the river. It was then used to battle {{TRT}} by using Psyshock on {{TP|Jessie|Woobat}}.

In [[PK22]], a {{pkmn2|wild}} Gothita, {{p|Drilbur}}, and {{p|Darumaka}} were angry at {{AP|Oshawott}} and {{AP|Tepig}}, who had eaten the apples that they had collected.

In ''[[BW055|Scraggy and the Demanding Gothita!]]'', [[Katharine]]'s Gothita developed a crush on {{AP|Scraggy}} and hoped he would join Katharine's [[Party|team]]. However, {{AP|Snivy}} defeated her in a battle, making her retreat.

In ''[[PK25|Eevee & Friends]]'', a Gothita and its evolved forms were friends with an {{p|Umbreon}} living with an {{p|Eevee}} and the rest of the [[Eeveelution]]s.

In ''[[PK27|Pikachu and the Pokémon Music Squad]]'', a Gothita was living in a forest.

In ''[[XY101|A Dancing Debut!]]'', [[Nini]] used a Gothita in the Theme Performance of the {{to|Couriway}} {{pkmn|Showcase}} alongside Nini's {{p|Smoochum}} and {{p|Farfetch'd}}. It helped Nini prepare a Poké Puff, advancing her to the Freestyle Performance. There, it delivered a performance along with its teammates, but it did not receive enough votes to win.

==Trivia==
* Gothita can be seen as a parallel to {{p|Solosis}}. Both are [[Generation V]] {{type|Psychic}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} evolving once at level 32 and then again at level 41. Gothita is [[Version-exclusive Pokémon|exclusive]] to Pokémon Black, Black 2, and Sword, while Solosis is exclusive to Pokémon White, White 2, and Shield. They both have the same base experience and catch rates, gain new moves at similar levels and have base stat totals of 290.

===Origin===
Gothita seems to be based on a child dressed in {{wp|Lolita fashion#Gothic Lolita|Gothic Lolita}} fashion, as well as the common trope to associate goth girls with magical powers or {{wp|witchcraft}}. The Gothic Lolita fashion itself is highly influenced by the {{wp|Victorian fashion|fashion from the Victorian period of the United Kingdom}} and also by styles from the {{wp|Rococo}} period, the Rococo being an artistic movement born in the 18th century in France that later spread to other parts of {{wp|Europe}} and the colonial {{wp|Americas}}.

Due to {{p|Gothitelle}}'s ability to predict the future from stars, Gothita and its Evolutions may be based on {{wp|Divination|diviners}}, or more specifically {{wp|Astrology|astrologers}}. Due to their feminine appearance and association with the stars, Gothita and its Evolutions may be inspired by the {{wp|Virgo (astrology)|Virgo sign}} and {{wp|Virgo (constellation)|constellation}}. This is further supported by {{p|Gothorita}} being the Virgo equivalent in Unova's [[Horoscope]].  

Gothita was featured in an official Pokémon illustration for the Year of the Ox in 2021.<ref>[https://x.com/poke_times/status/1346984961790906368 Gothita in the Chinese Zodiac art on official Pokémon X account]</ref> This is likely a pun involving its Japanese category name, ぎょうしポケモン ''Gyoushi Pokémon'' (Staring Pokémon) and うし ''ushi'', a common reading of 牛 (cow).

====Name origin====
Gothita may be derived from ''{{wp|Goth subculture|gothic}}'' and the Spanish feminine diminutive suffix ''-ita''. It may also be a contraction of ''Gothic lolita''.

Gothimu may be a combination of ''gothic'' and むちむち ''muchimuchi'' (chubby). It may also involve 予知夢 ''yochimu'' (precognitive dream).
