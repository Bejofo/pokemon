'''Skitty''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''エネコ''' ''Eneco'') is a {{type|Normal}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation III]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s into {{p|Delcatty}} when exposed to a [[Moon Stone]].

==Biology==
Skitty is a pink, feline {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a stumpy body and four short legs. On its face is a cream-colored, crescent-shaped marking. It has slit-like eyes and tufted ears with purple interiors. Its slender tail has a bulky pink section at the end, terminated by three pin-like structures with yellow ball-like tips. In battle, its tail puffs out and it threatens opponents with a sharp growl.

It is fascinated by moving objects and chases them. It is known to chase its own tail and become dizzy from it. Although it can be difficult to earn its trust, it is very popular due to its adorable looks and behavior. In [[Generation III]], {{m|Assist}} was its [[signature move]]. It lives in {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Forest Pokémon|holes in trees in tropical and temperate forests}}.

Skitty and its evolved form, {{p|Delcatty}}, are the [[Signature Ability|only known Pokémon]] that can have {{a|Normalize}} as an [[Ability]].

==Trivia==
* The [[Fluffy Tail]] item resembles Skitty's tail. This is because the Japanese name of the item is called ''{{tt|Eneco's Tail|エネコのシッポ}}'', ''Eneco'' being the Japanese name for Skitty.
* Skitty is the only {{cat|Generation III Pokémon}} that evolves via [[Moon Stone]].

* Despite Skitty and Delcatty being available in Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl, its former [[signature move]], {{m|Assist}}, is still unusable as of [[Generation VIII]].

===Origin===
Skitty appears to be based on a {{wp|kitten}} with its tail resembling a cat toy, which is also what the item [[Fluffy Tail]] appears to be. The tail also resembles a {{wp|Foxtail (diaspore)|foxtail grass}} or a pin holder. Skitty's crescent moon shaped face may allude to rich nocturnal life of cats. Its short legs in comparison to its body are comparable to {{wp|munchkin cat}}s.

====Name origin====
Skitty may be a combination of ''skittish'' and ''kitty''.

Eneco may be a combination of 狗尾草 ''enokorogusa'' ({{wp|Setaria viridis|green foxtail}}, a type of plant that Skitty's tail resembles) and 猫 ''neko'' (cat).
