'''Duosion''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ダブラン''' ''Doublan'') is a {{type|Psychic}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Solosis}} starting at [[level]] 32 and evolves into {{p|Reuniclus}} starting at level 41.

==Biology==
Duosion is a pale green {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with a large head, stubby arms, and a body that tapers to a point at the bottom. It has oval, black eyes, and a red, diamond-shaped mouth. It is encased in a darker green, gelatinous substance that forms small, round bumps on the lower sides and over Duosion's head. A small circular piece of matter floats inside this substance over this Pokémon's head. It has two brains, which think independently, causing it to attempt two different actions at once. However, when its brains think alike, it exhibits greater psychic powers, which are said to be able to travel half a mile away.

===Evolution===
Duosion evolves from {{p|Solosis}} and evolves into {{p|Reuniclus}}.

==Trivia==
* Duosion can be seen as a counterpart to {{p|Gothorita}}. Both are [[Generation V]] {{type|Psychic}} Pokémon that are the second stage of a three-stage evolutionary line with first [[Evolution]] at level 32 and second at level 41. Duosion is [[Version-exclusive Pokémon|exclusive]] to Pokémon White, White 2, and Shield, while Gothorita is exclusive to Pokémon Black, Black 2, and Sword. They both have the catch rate and they gain new moves at similar levels. However, Duosion's base stat total is 20 points lower than Gothorita's, though the other stages in its line have base stat totals that equal their counterparts'.

===Origin===
Duosion and the rest of its evolutionary line seem to bear reference to {{wp|Cell (biology)|cells}}, with the green substance surrounding their bodies resembling {{wp|cytoplasm}} and the objects inside their bodies representing {{wp|organelle}}s. They may also represent the process of {{wp|mitosis}}, as hinted by their Pokémon categories and the fact the line down their head also resembles a {{wp|cleavage furrow}}. It may also make allusion to the {{wp|Embryonic development|embryonary stages}}, specifically when the embryo is fully developed to become into a {{wp|fetus}}, while the green jelly may represent the {{wp|amniotic fluid}}.

====Name origin====
Duosion may be a combination of ''duo'' (two) and ''{{wp|fission (biology)|fission}}'', ''{{wp|cell division|division}}'', or ''{{wp|Cell fusion|fusion}}''.

Doublan may be a combination of ''double'' and 卵 ''ran'' ({{wp|egg cell}}).
