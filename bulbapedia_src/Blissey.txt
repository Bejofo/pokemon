'''Blissey''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ハピナス''' ''Happinas'') is a {{type|Normal}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation II]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Chansey}} when [[friendship Evolution|leveled up with high friendship]]. It is the final form of {{p|Happiny}}.

(Specifics may differ in past games. Refer to [[#Evolution data|Game data&rarr;Evolution data]] for these details.)

==Biology==
Blissey is a {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} that has a large, ovoid body with a pink upper body and white lower body. It has hair-like curls on either side of its head. White, wing-like tufts grow from its hips and shoulders, creating the impression of a dress. Its stubby arms and feet are pink and lack any digits. A small, pink pouch on its belly holds a single egg, which brings feelings of happiness and makes a person pleasant and caring if ingested.

The egg can cause a person to start acting kindly to others and calm down even the most ferocious Pokémon. Eating a Blissey's [[Lucky Egg|egg]] is said to bring good fortune to those who have consumed it. Blissey's egg can fetch the highest price at markets. Blissey can use its fluffy fur to sense sadness and will rush to anyone feeling sad or ill and attempt to nurse them back to health. Blissey is said to be overflowing with love. Blissey is a female-only species with no male counterpart. An {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Urban Pokémon|urban Pokémon}}, it is rarely seen in the wild. In {{g|Legends: Arceus}}, if the player is injured, any nearby Blissey will approach the player and will wait nearby, only fleeing after the player has recovered to full health.<ref>https://x.com/Sibuna_Switch/status/1827900892114190371</ref> As mentioned in [[Sleep style|Sleep Style Dex]], Blissey has sometimes been observed hugging its Lucky Egg while sleeping. It is believed Blissey is possibly dreaming about tending to its egg. Blissey has four different sleep styles it sleeps in: ''Seated Sleep'', ''Wobbling Sleep'', ''Egg-Minding Sleep'', and ''Atop-Belly Sleep''.


Blissey and its pre-evolved forms are the [[signature move|only known Pokémon]] capable of learning the move {{m|Soft-Boiled}} by leveling up.

===Evolution===
Blissey evolves from {{p|Chansey}} and is the final form of {{p|Happiny}}.

===Major appearances===
====[[Nurse Joy's Blissey]]====
Blissey debuted in ''[[EP129|Ignorance is Blissey]]'', where a Blissey belonging to [[Happy Town]] [[Nurse Joy]] was revealed to be a friend of [[Jessie]]'s from their time at the [[Pokémon Nurse School]]. Additionally, Nurse Joys in the [[Alola]] [[region]] are assisted by Blissey. The Nurse Joy of [[Fula City]] also has a Blissey in ''[[M21|The Power of Us]]'', which is set in [[I Choose You! trilogy|an alternate timeline]] from the main series.

====[[Brock's Blissey]]====
Brock's Chansey was shown to have evolved into a Blissey in ''[[JNS02|The Arceus Chronicles (Part 2)]]''.

==Trivia==
* Blissey has the highest base {{stat|HP}} and {{stat|Special Defense}} stats of all {{type|Normal}} Pokémon, and is tied with {{p|Eternatus|Eternamax Eternatus}} for the highest base HP stat of all Pokémon.
** A level 100 Dynamaxed Blissey with 31 IVs in HP, 252 EVs in HP, and a Dynamax Level of 10 has the highest HP of all Pokémon at 1428 HP.
** Blissey has the highest base HP of all Pokémon in {{g|GO}}, at 496.
* With a base experience yield of 635 (608 prior to [[Generation VIII]]), Blissey has the largest experience yield of any Pokémon.
** Blissey is also the only Pokémon whose base experience yield is greater than its base stat total.
* Blissey shares its {{pkmn|category}} with {{p|Togetic}}. They are both known as the Happiness Pokémon.

===Origin===
Blissey appears to be based on a nurse, specifically sharing some physical similarities with [[Nurse Joy]] from the {{pkmn|animated series}} in both color and hair. Its tendency to carry eggs in a pouch is much like a {{wp|monotreme}} (mainly the {{wp|echidna}}) or a {{wp|marsupial}}, but may also simply be based on a general concept of females and motherhood. Its names suggest it may be based on literal happiness.

====Name origin====
Blissey may be a combination of ''bliss'' (happiness) and the suffix ''-sey'' from its pre-Evolution ''{{p|Chansey}}''.

Happinas may be a combination of ''happiness'' and ''nurse''. It may also involve 茄子 ''nasu'' ({{wp|eggplant}}), referring to the egg theme of this evolutionary line.
