'''Stufful''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ヌイコグマ''' ''Nuikoguma'') is a dual-type {{2t|Normal|Fighting}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation VII]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Bewear}} starting at [[level]] 27.

==Biology==
Stufful is a small, quadrupedal {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} resembling a bear cub. Its body and head are mostly pink, while its legs are brown. Each foot has a single pink paw pad on the bottom. There is a white, semicircle marking around its face similar to a {{wp|headband}}, and it has a white muzzle with a triangular, black nose. Its white-tipped ears are situated on the sides of its head, and there is a small, white-tipped tuft of fur under each. Around the center of its short, puffy tail is a dark pink ring marking. Under the tail is a small, white flap resembling the tag on a stuffed toy. This is actually an organ that releases an odor used to communicate with others of its kind.

Despite its small size, Stufful has extraordinary strength and can knock out even well-trained Pokémon if they are unprepared. The combination of its cute appearance, mannerisms, and fluffy fur make it very popular.<ref>[http://www.pokemon-sunmoon.com/en-us/pokemon/stufful/ Pokémon Sun and Moon site | Stufful]</ref> However, Stufful actually dislikes being touched and will flail its powerful arms if it is embraced. Stufful's flailing is powerful enough to break thick tree trunks. As mentioned in the [[Sleep style|Sleep Style Dex]], Stufful is filled with power despite sleeping. It is often seen flailing about and moving constantly while sleeping. As in the [[Poké Problem]] segment of ''[[SM124|Living on the Cutting Edge!]]'', Stufful is powerful enough to break through walls of steel.

Prior to [[Generation VIII]], Stufful and its evolved form, {{p|Bewear}}, were the [[Signature Ability|only known Pokémon]] that could have {{a|Fluffy}} as an [[Ability]].

=====[[Stufful (anime)]]=====
Stufful debuted in ''[[SM044|A Dream Encounter!]]'', where it was seen in a video advertising [[Aether Paradise]]. It physically appeared in ''[[SM046|Deceiving Appearances!]]''. In ''[[SM096|Don't Ignore the Small Stufful!]]'', it was revealed to be the baby of {{an|Bewear}}, [[Team Rocket trio|Team Rocket]]'s de facto caretaker, and was reunited with its mother by the end of the episode.

=====Other=====
A {{pkmn|Trainer}}'s Stufful appeared in ''[[SM128|League Offenders and Defenders!]]''. Team Rocket encountered it and mistook it for Bewear's child, therefore believing its presence to be a sign of Bewear being on her way to take them home. ==Trivia==
* Stufful and its Evolution are the first, and so far only, Pokémon evolutionary line to make their [[Pokémon GO]] debut in a [[Community Day]].

===Origin===
Stufful appears to be based on the {{wp|red panda}}, as well as stuffed animals such as {{wp|teddy bear}}s. The white flail under its tail also resembles the tag seen on stuffed animal toys.

====Name origin====
Stufful may be derived from ''stuffed animal'' or a combination of ''stuff'' and ''full''. It may also incorporate ''scuffle'', for its aggressive nature and propensity to fight.

Nuikoguma may be a combination of ぬいぐるみ ''nuigurumi'' (stuffed animal) and 子熊 ''koguma'' (bear cub).
