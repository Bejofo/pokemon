'''Darumaka''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ダルマッカ''' ''Darumakka'') is a {{type|Fire}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Darmanitan}} starting at [[level]] 35.

In [[Galar]], Darumaka has an {{type|Ice}} [[regional form]]. It evolves into Galarian Darmanitan when exposed to an [[Ice Stone]].

==Biology==
[[File:Darumaka sleeping.png|thumb|left|250px|Darumaka asleep]]
Darumaka is a round, red {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} with short arms and legs and a stubby tail. It has an orange face and curly yellow eyebrows. It has three yellow ovals on its abdomen and a curvy yellow line on its back. Its hands and feet are orange with three digits each.

Darumaka has a flame inside its body. While this flame is burning, Darumaka is very active. It cannot calm down in this state, and thus becomes uncontrollable. As the flame shrinks, it falls asleep; at rest, its internal temperature is about 1,100&nbsp;°F (600&nbsp;°C). It tucks in its arms and legs when it rests; during that time, it slightly sways. While it is sleeping, it cannot be tipped over no matter how much it is pushed or pulled, like a {{wp|Tumbler toy|tumbler doll}}, which it resembles in this state. Darumaka is well known as a symbol of good luck. It is also known to have very warm [[Pokémon food#Pokémon droppings|droppings]], which ancient people put in their clothes to keep themselves warm. It prefers to live in a desert environment.

===Forms===
Darumaka has a [[regional form]]: Galarian Darumaka.

{{rf|Galarian}} Darumaka's snow-white coat is thicker than that of Unovan Darumaka. It has a pair of turquoise ice crystals in place of its eyebrows. Instead of oval-shaped markings, a pair of diamond-shaped ice crystals adorn its chest like buttons. Its face, hands, and feet are blue rather than orange. Unlike its Unovan counterpart, Galarian Darumaka has a timid frown instead of a mischievous smile.

Seismic shift in the [[Galar]] region long ago caused the warm climate to cool, and Darumaka in this region have adapted to this cooling. The fire that burned within their bodies went out, leaving an organ that produces cold air instead. The colder Galarian Darumaka gets, the more energetic it becomes. They can focus their internal energy to breathe out cold air in the form of snow, which they use to make snowballs for ammo in playful snowball fights with each other.<ref>[https://swordshield.pokemon.com/en-us/pokemon-galar-region/darumaka/ Galarian Darumaka | Official Website | Pokémon Sword and Pokémon Shield]</ref> Galarian Darumaka enjoys eating {{Berries}} produced by {{p|Snover}}. This leads it to prey on Snover, often causing it to be driven off by {{p|Abomasnow}}.

==Trivia==
* Darumaka has the lowest {{stat|Special Attack}} base stat of all {{type|Fire}} Pokémon.
* In their debut games, {{game|Black and White|s}}, Darumaka lived in a [[Desert Resort|desert]] with constant [[Sandstorm (weather condition)|sandstorms]], despite having no [[type]]-based protection against sandstorm damage. In ''[[BW056|The Lonely Deino!]]'', Darumaka are shown to burrow under the sand, which may intend to clarify how they protect themselves in the sandstorm.

===Origin===
Darumaka is based on a {{wp|Daruma doll}}, and its {{t|Fire}} type is inspired by the traditional {{wp|Daruma doll#Daruma burning|Daruma burning ceremony}}.<ref name=ND204>''Nintendo Dream'' Vol. 204, April 2011 ([https://web.archive.org/web/20110704155314/http://shellspider.blog101.fc2.com/blog-entry-502.html Shellspider's blog (Japanese)], [http://lavacutcontent.com/ken-sugimori-nintendo-dream-2/ Translation by Dr. Lava])</ref> When it is resting, it resembles the {{wp|Roly-poly toy|type of Daruma doll that does not tip over}}.

{{rf|Galarian}} Darumaka may be based on a mixture of a {{wp|snowman}}, called 雪だるま ''yuki-daruma'' in Japanese, and a {{wp|yeti}}.

====Name origin====
Darumaka and Darumakka seem to be a combination of ''{{wp|Daruma doll|Daruma}}'' and 真赤 ''makka'' (crimson) or a corruption of ''{{wp|macaque}}''.
