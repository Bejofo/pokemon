'''Crustle''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''イワパレス''' ''Iwapalace'') is a dual-type {{2t|Bug|Rock}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Dwebble}} starting at [[level]] 34.

==Biology==
[[File:Crustle no shell.png|thumb|left|250px|Crustle without its rock]]
Crustle is an orange {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} resembling an insect or crustacean. Its most distinguishing feature is its enormous, hard shell, which resembles a cut-away block of sediment with multiple layers and strata visible. Crustle's shell grants it an {{a|Sturdy|impressive defense}} in combat. The tips of its pincers and six feet are dark gray. It has two glaring yellow eyes. Crustle possesses a straight tail that is concealed underneath its shell.

Its legs are very strong, to the point that it can carry heavy rocks for a long time, even through the arid lands where it resides. Due to this environment, a Crustle will not come out of its shell if it is a rainy day. It engages members of its species in territorial battles that can become especially vicious. If its shell breaks during such a fight, it loses. When Crustle's shell breaks, it will become weaker and unsure of itself. Crustle's biggest strength is its thick claws, which are strong enough to break even {{p|Rhyperior}}'s sturdy carapace.

===Evolution===
Crustle evolves from {{p|Dwebble}}.

===Major appearances===
====[[Cilan's Crustle]]====
In ''[[BW062|Crisis at Chargestone Cave!]]'', {{an|Cilan}}'s Dwebble evolved into a Crustle during a {{pkmn|battle}} against {{TRT}}.

====Other====
In ''[[BW103|Curtain Up, Unova League!]]'' and ''[[BW104|Mission: Defeat Your Rival!]]'', [[Ultimo]]'s Crustle battled [[Virgil]] during the [[Vertress Conference]]. It was defeated by his {{p|Vaporeon}}.

===Minor appearances===
In ''[[BW075|A Restoration Confrontation! Part 1]]'', a group of excavators used multiple Crustle while working in [[Twist Mountain]].

==Trivia==
* No other Pokémon has the same [[Egg Group]] combination as Crustle and its pre-evolved form.
* Crustle represents {{wp|July}} in the [[Unova]] [[horoscope]].
* Crustle is the only Pokémon in {{g|Shuffle}} that can take a maximum of 8 Raise Max Level {{DL|List of items in Pokémon Shuffle|Enhancements}}.

===Origin===
Crustle is based on a {{wp|hermit crab}}. Its shell resembles a block of {{wp|stratum}} rather than an actual shell, though the design also seems to resemble ornately-colored hermit crab shells.

====Name origin====
Crustle may be a combination of ''{{wp|Crust_(geology)|crust}}'', ''{{wp|crustacean}}'', ''crab'', and ''castle''.

Iwapalace may be a combination of 岩 ''iwa'' (rock) and ''palace''.
