'''Raboot''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ラビフット''' ''Rabbifuto'') is a {{type|Fire}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation VIII]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Scorbunny}} starting at [[level]] 16 and evolves into {{p|Cinderace}} starting at level 35.

==Biology==
Raboot is a humanoid, {{wp|rabbit}}-like {{OBP|Pokémon|species}}. It has thick, fluffy fur in multiple colors: the fur on its head, hands, and small tail are grayish-white, while the fur on its body is primarily black, with red colorations on its arms, shoulders, and front half of its feet. It also has a fur collar that covers its neck and mouth like a {{wp|Balaclava (clothing)|balaclava}}, while its belly has tufts that are similar in appearance and function to jacket pockets. Raboot also has long ears, a few tufts of fur on top of its head, red irises, and a small yellow nose. 

Raboot's fur protects it from the cold, and allows it to use hotter fire moves, which it delivers through the yellow marks on its forehead and soles.<ref name=SwShsite/> Raboot takes great pride in its kicking moves. Raboot usually practices its footwork by kicking {{Berries}} from a tree's branches and then juggling them with its feet. While training its kicking attacks, Raboot tries to avoid using its arms, tucking them under its fur. This effectively prevents Raboot from losing body heat.<ref name=SwShsite>[https://swordshield.pokemon.com/en-us/pokemon-galar-region/raboot/ Raboot | Official Website | Pokémon Sword and Pokémon Shield]</ref> Raboot is also capable of delivering powerful headbutts once its flames within heat up its forehead.

Raboot and its evolutionary relatives are the [[Signature Ability|only known Pokémon]] that can have {{a|Libero}} as an [[Ability]].

====Major appearances====
=====[[Goh's Raboot]]=====
Raboot debuted in ''[[JN017|Kicking It from Here Into Tomorrow!]]'', where it evolved from [[Goh]]'s Scorbunny. It had an aloof personality. In ''[[JN045|Sword and Shield... The Legends Awaken!]]'', it evolved into a Cinderace during a battle against [[Oleana]].

=====Other=====
In ''[[JN090|Showdown at the Gates of Warp!]]'', [[Alternate World Goh]]'s Cinderace [[Devolution|devolved]] into Raboot, then Scorbunny, and finally an {{pkmn|Egg}} due to {{OBP|Dialga|recurring}} and {{OBP|Palkia|recurring}}'s influence. It was later restored to normal.

==Trivia==
* Raboot and its pre-evolved form share their category with {{p|Buneary}} and {{p|Lopunny}}. They are all known as the Rabbit Pokémon.
* Raboot and its evolutionary relatives are the only pure Fire-type Pokémon introduced in [[Generation VIII]].
* Excluding {{p|Silvally}} holding a [[Fire Memory]], Raboot is the only {{DL|List of Pokémon by color|gray}} {{type|Fire}} Pokémon.

===Origin===
Raboot appears to be based on a {{wp|rabbit}}. It also has design elements that may be based on trainer outfits worn by athletes, such as a {{wp|tracksuit}} and {{wp|Headband#Utilitarian uses|headband}}.

====Name origin====
Raboot may be a combination of ''rabbit'', ''foot'', ''soot'', and ''boot''.

Rabbifuto may be a combination of ''rabbit'' and ''foot''.
