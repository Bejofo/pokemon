'''Roggenrola''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ダンゴロ''' ''Dangoro'') is a {{type|Rock}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] into {{p|Boldore}} starting at [[level]] 25, which evolves into {{p|Gigalith}} when [[Trade Evolution|traded]].

==Biology==
Roggenrola is a roughly spherical rock-like {{OBP|Pokémon|species}}. Its body is blue and covered in large, smooth facets. It has a hexagonal, yellow artifice in the center of its body, leading to a hexagonal opening that functions as an ear. Roggenrola has a brown, rocky oblong atop its head and similar brown rocks that serve as feet.

Roggenrola is born deep underground and was discovered about 100 years ago in an earthquake fissure in a layer of exposed rock. Its steel-hard body is made up of dirt from many centuries ago and it compares its {{a|Sturdy|hard physique}} with those of {{p|Geodude}} and {{p|Carbink}}. However, a sufficiently long soak in water will cause its body to soften to some degree. Inside of Roggenrola lies an energy core, making its body somewhat warm to the touch. Its ear leads straight to its energy core, causing it to become angry if someone sticks their hand in there. As stated by {{an|Cilan}} in ''[[BW035|Where Did You Go, Audino?]]'', Roggenrola has sensitive hearing and can hear sounds that are inaudible to humans. It tends to follow the sounds it picks up with its lone ear, panicking if these sounds cease. {{p|Dwebble}} is a natural enemy of Roggenrola, as the former tends to think that Roggenrola is an inanimate rock to turn into a home. Similarly, {{p|Rhyperior}} has been known to mistake this Pokémon for an ordinary rock and fire them through their hands.

===Evolution===
Roggenrola evolves into {{p|Boldore}}, which evolves into {{p|Gigalith}}.

==In animation==
===Main series===
[[File:Goh Roggenrola.png|thumb|250px|Roggenrola in {{aniseries|JN}}]]
====Major appearances====
=====[[Ash's Roggenrola]]=====
Roggenrola debuted in ''[[BW034|Gotta Catch A Roggenrola!]]'', where it was {{pkmn2|caught}} by Ash by the end of the episode. It later evolved into a Boldore in ''[[BW061|Battling the King of the Mines!]]''.

=====Other=====
In ''Gotta Catch A Roggenrola!'', fourteen Roggenrola were targeted by Team Rocket. However, they were rescued by Ash and his friends, with the help of a fifteenth Roggenrola who was part of their group. They reappeared in a flashback in ''[[BW064|Explorers of the Hero's Ruin!]]''.

In ''[[SM070|The Young Flame Strikes Back!]]'', one of [[Viren's henchmen]] used a Roggenrola to help intimidate {{an|Kiawe}} and his family, but it was defeated by Kiawe's {{rf|Alolan}} {{TP|Kiawe|Marowak}}.

Multiple Roggenrola appeared in ''[[JN080|Trial on a Golden Scale!]]'', with {{DL|List of Goh's Pokémon|Roggenrola|one}} of them being caught by [[Goh]].

====Minor appearances====
A Roggenrola appeared in a flashback in ''[[BW131|The Path That Leads to Goodbye!]]''.

Multiple Roggenrola appeared in ''[[BW136|A Pokémon of a Different Color!]]''.

In ''[[BW138|Go, Go Gogoat!]]'', a Roggenrola was living at an {{OBP|unnamed island|BW138}} in the [[Decolore Islands]].

Four {{pkmn|Trainer}}s' Roggenrola appeared in ''[[SM006|A Shocking Grocery Run!]]''.

==Trivia==
[[File:Shadow Roggenrola.png|thumb|100px|right|Shadow Roggenrola]]
* Roggenrola is similar to {{p|Geodude}}: both are {{type|Rock}}, have {{a|Sturdy}} as one their {{Abilities}}, evolve at level 25, and reach their final forms when [[trade]]d.
* Roggenrola is tied with {{p|Rockruff}} and {{p|Nacli}} for the lowest base stat total of all Rock-type Pokémon.
* In [[Unova]], there is a televised version of bowling that uses Roggenrola as balls. There is a chance of explosion, depending on the Roggenrola.
* Roggenrola is the only [[Generation V]] {{DL|List of Pokémon by shape|Pokémon consisting of a head and legs}}.
*Although Roggenrola's hexagonal cavity is its ear, and not an eye, it still turns red if it is a {{GO|Shadow Pokémon}}.

===Origin===
Roggenrola appears to be based on a {{wp|geode}} and {{wp|elemental}} creature, specifically a being of earth.

====Name origin====
Roggenrola may be derived from ''rock'' and ''{{wp|rock and roll}}'' or ''rock-and-roller''.

Dangoro may be a combination of 団子 ''{{wp|dango}}'' (Japanese dumpling) and ゴロゴロ ''goro goro'' (onomatopoeia for the sound of a boulder rolling).
