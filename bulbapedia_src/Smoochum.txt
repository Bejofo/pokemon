'''Smoochum''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''ムチュール''' ''Muchul'') is a dual-type {{2t|Ice|Psychic}} [[baby Pokémon]] introduced in [[Generation II]].

It [[Evolution|evolve]]s into {{p|Jynx}} starting at [[level]] 30.

==Biology==
Smoochum is a small, {{wp|humanoid}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} that resembles a baby. It has a large head with blonde hair that reaches its shoulders and has large bangs. There are several tufts of hair sticking up on top of its head. Its skin is light purple with a cream-colored chest, arms, and feet. It has large, blue-green eyes and a pair of large, pink lips. These lips are sensitive enough that Smoochum uses them to identify and examine objects that it sees for the first time, and are kept moist by its daily application of tree sap. This help Smoochum determine if it likes the object or not.

When walking, Smoochum rocks its head back and forth. Smoochum is known to be very curious. This Pokémon is very active and runs about, although it often falls. Being very conscious of its appearance, it will check its reflection when given the chance to make sure it is not dirty. It will bathe the instant it finds any dirt on its face. However, it doesn't mind if its body get dirty. Smoochum is found living in {{DL|List of Pokémon by habitat|Urban Pokémon|cities}} and cold caverns. Smoochum is a female-only species, with no male counterpart.

===Major appearances===
Smoochum debuted in ''[[PK07|Pikachu & Pichu]]'' as one of the best friends of the [[Pichu Brothers]]. She made further appearances in ''[[Trouble in Big Town]]'', ''[[HS06|Of Meowth]] [[HS07|and Pokémon]]'', ''[[HS09|Big Meowth,]] [[HS10|Little Dreams]]'', and ''[[Pichu Bros. in Party Panic]]''. She also made a brief cameo appearance in ''[[M11|Giratina and the Sky Warrior]]''.

Smoochum made her animated series debut in ''[[EP203|The Screen Actor's Guilt]]'', under the ownership of [[Brad Van Darn]] and the [[nickname]] of Smoochy. Brad's agent was afraid that his reputation as an action star would be ruined should his Smoochum be discovered.

In ''[[AG168|Three Jynx and a Baby]]'', a Smoochum lived with her three {{p|Jynx}} sisters in [[Cremini Town]]. She became separated and stumbled upon {{TRT}}. [[Jessie]] had planned on using Smoochum's cuteness for profit, but she was rescued by the Jynx, {{Ash}}, and {{ashfr}}. She later evolved into Jynx at the end of the episode.

In ''[[DP088|Camping It Up!]]'', Jessie was provided with a {{DL|List of Pokémon temporarily owned by the Team Rocket trio|Smoochum}} to train at the [[Pokémon Summer Academy]].

In [[PK20]] A Smoochum alongside [[Spiky-eared Pichu|Pichu]] was organizing a birthday party for a {{p|Bellossom}}, but winded up losing her birthday gift due to an accident involving [[Ash's Pikachu|Pikachu]] and [[Dawn's Piplup|Piplup]]. With their help they search for the lost jewels.

In ''[[DP190|The Brockster Is In!]]'', [[Normajean]]'s Smoochum was badly poisoned by a {{p|Tentacruel}} alongside several of her other baby Pokémon. She was then healed by a {{m|Softboiled}} from [[Brock's Chansey]].

In ''[[XY050|Pathways to Performance Partnering!]]'', [[Nini]] owns a Smoochum who had a crush on [[Serena's Pancham]]. She reappeared in ''[[XY091|A Performance Pop Quiz!]]'', ''[[XY101|A Dancing Debut!]]'', and ''[[XY112|Master Class Is in Session!]]''.

In ''[[PK27|Pikachu and the Pokémon Music Squad]]'', Pancham and {{TP|Clemont|Chespin}} both had a crush on a Smoochum. As a result, they competed for her feelings before {{TP|Serena|Braixen}} put an end to it.

In ''[[M21|The Power of Us]]'', a Smoochum was following [[Harriet]] around.

===Origin===
[[File:Spr 2d 218.png|thumb|200px|Smoochum's ''Spaceworld '97'' design.]]
<!-- Ganguro wasn't a fashion style come Pokemon Red and Green and some Jynx designs (eg. in Tajiri's popularity poll) outright predate its inception, so it seems more likely that Jynx and Smoochum are just based on gyaru in general. -->
Smoochum may be based on ギャル ''{{wp|gyaru}}'', blonde-haired, tanned Japanese girls, but is meant to resemble an otherwise normal human child. One type of gyaru, the [https://japantoday.com/category/features/lifestyle/meet-aki-queen-of-the-gyaru-mama gyaru-mama], will raise their children with the gyaru fashion style, usually in their image. This concept is more visibly seen in its design from the [[Pokémon Gold and Silver Spaceworld '97 demo]], where Smoochum more clearly takes after Jynx. Additionally, {{wp|Yuki-onna}}, a possible origin for {{p|Jynx}}, is sometimes shown as holding a seemingly human child. Smoochum could also be based on the legend of {{wp|Kintarō}}, a human child raised by the {{wp|Yama-uba}}, another possible origin of Jynx.

Smoochum's habit of kissing things to learn through taste and touch is a behaviour seen in babies and toddlers, which often bite things or hold them in their mouth for similar reasons.

====Name origin====
Smoochum may be derived from ''smooch'' (a {{wp|kiss}}) and possibly ''chum'' (friend). It may also be a pun on "smooch 'em".

Muchul may be a combination of 夢中 ''muchū'' (daze or trance) and ちゅう ''chu'' (the sound of a kiss).
