'''Accelgor''' ([[List of Japanese Pokémon names|Japanese]]: '''アギルダー''' ''Agilder'') is a {{type|Bug}} {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} introduced in [[Generation V]].

It [[Evolution|evolves]] from {{p|Shelmet}} when [[Trade Evolution|traded]] for a {{p|Karrablast}}, a Pokémon that evolves into {{p|Escavalier}} simultaneously. Neither evolves if at least one holds an [[Everstone]] during the trade.

==Biology==
Accelgor is a {{OBP|Pokémon|species}} which has a large pink head reminiscent of a {{wp|helmet}} that is curled in the back. Green stripes run from its eyes to the top of its head, and there is a black star-like marking or hole on the front. Its eyes are hidden in a shadowed crevice in its head. The rest of its body is covered by a blue, cloth-like membrane, which keeps Accelgor from dehydrating, with ribbon-like extensions at the top of its back. Its arms, when crossed, portray an aggressive mood.

It is a very fast Pokémon and attacks in ways similar to ninjas. In [[Generation VIII]], {{m|Water Shuriken}} was its [[signature move]]. It lives in moist habitats like bogs and swamps. Accelgor is often featured as a main character in movies or comics to increase the popularity of these products. Accelgor's pouting expression and crossed arms are due to it being miserable over having lost its shell.<ref>''Nintendo Dream'' Vol. 201, January 2011 ([http://lavacutcontent.com/ken-sugimori-nintendo-dream-3/ Translation by Dr. Lava])</ref>

===Major appearances===
Accelgor debuted in ''[[BW057|The Mighty Accelguard to the Rescue!]]'', under the ownership of [[Charles]]. Charles used Accelgor during [[Dr. Ferrara|Cryogonal Man]]'s attempt to rob the [[Cold Storage]], during which Accelgor showed off its abilities. It was able to defeat Cryogonal Man's powerful {{p|Vanillish}} in a {{pkmn|battle}}.

In ''[[BW063|Evolution Exchange Excitement!]]'', {{an|Bianca}}'s {{p|Shelmet}} evolved into an Accelgor after being traded with [[Professor Juniper]] for her {{p|Karrablast}}.

===Minor appearances===
An Accelgor appeared in a fantasy in ''[[BW062|Crisis at Chargestone Cave!]]''.

In ''[[XY109|Master Class Choices!]]'', [[Shulin]]'s Accelgor was seen participating in the [[Fleurrh City]] [[Pokémon Showcase]] alongside {{p|Escavalier}}. They had to round up the most {{p|Rhyhorn}} and were able to do so, thus making it into the Freestyle Performance. In ''[[XY112|Master Class Is in Session!]]'', it was used in the {{ci|Gloire}} Master Class Showcase.

==Trivia==

===Origin===
Accelgor seems to be based on an {{wp|Stock car racing|auto}} {{wp|drag racing|racer}} or a {{wp|ninja}}, combined with a {{wp|slug}}, {{wp|snail}}, or {{wp|giant tube worm}}. Its cloth-like "clothing" and {{t|Bug}} type suggest it may also be based on the {{wp|bagworm moth|bagworm}} pupa.

====Name origin====
Accelgor may be a combination of ''accelerate'' and ''escargot'' (French for snail).

Agilder may be a combination of ''agile'' and ''rider''.
